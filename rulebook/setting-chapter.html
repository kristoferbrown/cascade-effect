Executive Abstract

  • Individuals that are capable of utilizing a controlled cascade effect to gain abilities that appear supernatural exist.
  • These abilities, referred to as styles, are powered by Entanglement, a measure of how connected a person is to the Yau.
  • Normal people can be disabled by severing their connection to the Yau, but people with Styles instead only lose a portion of their Entanglement.
  • Numerous organizations of people with styles exist. The details of these groups, their goals, and dossiers of their leaders are presented at the end of this section.

Entangled Individuals

Throughout the world, people with the ability to create a self-sustaining cascade effect in order to manifest seemingly superhuman abilities exist. These abilities are referred to as Styles, and the people that are able to learn them do so by causing a cascade of information to travel between their body and a spatial phenomenon known as the Yau. The degree to which a person is connected to the Yau is referred to as Entanglement. Further details on the nature of the Yau are classified CONTEXT SHANNON and not in the purview of this section.

People with a heightened connection to the Yau have always existed. In fact, it's plausible that many historical figures that are viewed as extremely competent may have had some measure of Entanglement or styles. It is difficult to measure how prevalent these individuals currently are, but estimates predict that they make up approximately 0.01% of the global population. There is also evidence that this rate has varied greatly throughout history and has risen significantly in recent years.

There does not appear to be any demographic or geographic pattern to the emergence of entangled people. However, exposure to cascade events, dissociative episodes, and other entangled individuals all correlate with new people becoming entangled.

Degrees of Entanglement

The level to which an entangled person can manifest abilities varies greatly. People can be sorted by their degree of capability into three increasingly powerful and increasingly rare categories; entangled individuals, hyper-competents, and people with full access to styles.

The entangled are the most common group. They have a small measure of Entanglement but no knowledge or ability to do anything with it. These people do not have any Styles and likely will never learn any, but their increased Entanglement makes them more resistant to cascade events and being disconnected from the Yau. Some of the Entangled are capable of using the basic Entanglement capabilities of Technique and Recover, however they are typically not aware of this ability and instead just feel like they can focus extra hard on a task to excel at it.

Hyper-competent people are less common than the Entangled. These people have a few styles, but only styles that improve their attributes and skills somewhat. Most of them are incapable of improving beyond the normal limit imposed by the human body, and thus they still only have attributes and skills with a maximum of 3 points and they cannot learn any specialty or auxiliary styles. These people do not appear to have any superhuman abilities, they just appear to be very competent in many fields and extremely successful in their area of expertise.

Of all the people with a connection to the Yau, the least common group are those that are capable of learning any style. These extremely rare people have true mastery over their Entanglement and can use styles to become truly superhuman. If the estimate that all entangled individuals make up 0.01% of the population is accurate, then individuals capable of superhuman styles likely only make up approximately one to two percent of those with Entanglement.

Experiencing Entanglement

People with mastery over their Entanglement experience it as a resource that can be accumulated and expended to fuel styles, and styles are experienced as the superhuman powers that can be granted by Entanglement. These experiences shed little light on the actual phenomena that allow them to be possible, and as such many people with styles utilize a variety of different terms and conceptual frameworks to attempt to understand and describe them.

At the most fundamental level, Entanglement is just a measure of the intensity and potential bandwidth of the connection between a person's physical body and the portion of their consciousness that resides in the Yau called the Yau body. As styles are activated a portion of this connection is earmarked for running, maintaining, and recovering from that style. Eventually that portion of the connection will be freed up and available for active use again. This relationship results in the sensation that Entanglement can be used as a resource, burned up, or expended until it slowly regenerates.

Entanglement Acquisition

Normally, people with Entanglement have a point at which their Entanglement is in balance. They will slowly gain or lose Entanglement over time until they are at this point. However, certain circumstances may greatly accelerate this process.

Before Orthodynamic Field Theory postulated an explanation for what Entanglement is, many people with styles referred to Entanglement as Crisis. This name was given because people can sometimes experience sudden gains in the amount of Entanglement they have available when they are involved in high tension, risky, dynamic, or fast moving situations.

This phenomenon happens because fast moving objects and objects with high potential energy cause the shallow Yau to become blurry and less defined. The greater the state of flux in the nearby Yau, the easier it is for a Yau body to create connections. When an area with static, unmoving, and heavily defined Yau structures suddenly bursts into action the Yau body subconsciously takes advantage of the new found ease of forging connections and will attempt to create as much Entanglement as possible.

Properties of Styles

On the surface, styles are a measure of how experienced a person is at using their Entanglement. As a person gains more styles, a greater portion of their mind resides inside their Yau body instead of their brain. This has the effect of giving the person a greater degree of conscious control over the potential powers their Entanglement can offer. The more self-aware your Yau body is the larger your repertoire of styles can be. Exporting a portion of your consciousness into the Yau is not without side effects, however. Large numbers of styles tend to make people seem less human, less connected to reality, and more prone to experiencing dissociative episodes.

Styles work by increasing the computational capabilities of the Yau body and by allowing the exchange of stored resources and information between the Yau and physical space. Theoretically, styles could allow you to directly change any particle or force into anything other, but the process of doing something that precise is extremely difficult to comprehend. Because of the challenge of comprehension, people can only utilize specific styles that they have incrementally gained a deeply intuitive understanding of. It is much easier to enhance the human body's natural attributes and capabilities to greater levels than it is to directly influence reality.

Typically the Yau body is little more than an external computer that supplements the brain. Styles that improve mental attributes work simply by increasing the size and processing power of the Yau body. They make a person seem smarter and wittier by literally making them think faster, retain more information, and store more detailed memories. In addition to improved mental capabilities, a Yau body can utilize it's diffuse unbounded nature to reach out and gain empathic information about nearby Yau bodies and material information about nearby surroundings to increase attributes like charisma and perception.

Information entering physical space from the Yau also has the side effect of locally reversing entropy. This anti-entropic property is powerful enough to perform thermodynamic work, allowing people with styles to improve physical attributes. Attributes like Agility and Finesse both experience benefits from increased mental processing speed, but also from using the anti-entropic cascade to manipulate the position of the body. Attributes like Strength and Stamina can be greatly enhanced by this process and also by storing biological resources in the Yau, such as oxygen, adenosine triphosphate, blood clotting factors, and even entire red blood cells or extra muscle mass, and accessing them on demand.

Arcane styles function in the most complex way of all, and as such are typically more difficult to acquire because of this. These styles function by allowing you to directly influence the three fundamental forces, electromagnetism, gravitonertia, and chromodynamism. Theoretically, arcane styles could grant you the ability to rewrite any particle or force with anything else, however, the difficulty in comprehending the process of doing so only permits these styles to function in certain intuitively primal ways.

Dissociation

For a normal person, the Yau body is typically an just an unconscious calculator and a long-term memory storage, but for a person with Entanglement the increased connectivity can result in the Yau Body becoming more than that. The greater the number of connections a person has, the greater the portion of consciousness and self-awareness their Yau body processes. One of the consequences of having a consciousness divided between two organs that are not always consistently connected is varying types of dissociative episodes.

A common trigger of a dissociative episode is cascade events. These events add a directional pressure to information traveling either from or to the Yau. This pressure can trigger dissociative experiences in anyone. Some events are even powerful enough to sever someone's link to the Yau entirely. If this happens to a normal person, they will immediately fall unconscious and remain so until their connection has recovered. If this happens to a person with Entanglement, they will instead lose some of their Entganglement and have a dissociative episode and remain conscious.

At one point or another, almost everyone with Entanglement experiences some level of dissociation. This most frequently happens when the current Entanglement total is well above or well below the person's normal Entanglement maximum. A very low total will result in a surreal and depressing experience of depersonalization and of the feeling of being an external witness to one's own body. Alternatively, Entanglement levels well above the limit result in anxiety, mania, and a feeling of derealization where the world no longer seems real or that you are no longer a part of the real world. Certain cascade events can even result in a person's brain and Yau body getting out of sync resulting in a second internal monologue, in extreme cases this second voice can even be experienced as an auditory hallucination or a second personality.

While alarming, dissociative episodes are only the beginning of some of the strange things that can happen as a result of a self-aware Yau body. People with Entanglement that have not yet become familiar enough with it to have styles sometimes experience hypnogogic out of body experiences because they truly do have a certain amount of consciousness residing outside of their body. These sensations are sometimes the first hint that a person is capable of learning styles.

Organizations

Despite only being a tiny fraction of the population, people with styles tend to find trouble and they tend to find each other. While many historical movements, organizations, states, and political parties are sometimes thought to have been led by entangled individuals, there is no way to find evidence to support this. Conversely, several organizations arising in recent years have been infiltrated by intelligence agencies and conclusively shown to be founded by, led by, or composed primarily of entangled or styled individuals. While many of these groups are publicly known organizations, the nature of their ties to entangled individuals is classified as OCTET CYAN. As such, these groups are closely monitored and deeply infiltrated by international secret intelligence agents in order to prevent leaks of the knowledge of OCTET CYAN and CONTEXT SHANNON information.

The New Frontier

The New Frontier is the group that originally named and popularized the Postnational Frontier movement. The group was originally founded with the intention to remove people from the panopticon created by created by modern surveillance and national RFID programs, free them from wage-labor, a concept they view as equivalent to slavery, and to establish an anarcho-socialist utopia. There are four New Frontier settlements across western North America and one in Inner Mongolia. Each settlement looks more like a cross between Burning Man and a ramshackle solar plant than a traditional city. Every person contributing to a settlement is provided for to the best of the rest of the settlement's ability in an ad-hoc fashion.

Their extreme leftist worldview is still a very small minority, but it is rapidly gaining mainstream popularity due to the perceived success of their frontier settlements. Whether these settlements are actually a success or not is a matter of some debate, but it is certainly true that they would not have been possible without the advent of three key technologies: cheap solar panels fabrication, solid state water condensers, and automated FPGA logistics and distribution algorithms. These technologies have enabled the development of low-cost and theoretically sustainable communities in regions with little to no economic potential. By settling in these remote and worthless areas, The New Frontier has evaded attention from law enforcement and has been allowed to develop mostly unmolested.

While most information about The New Frontier is public knowledge, the true nature of some of its members is classified as OCTET CYAN. First, its founder, known only by the pseudonym Delano, made extensive use of advanced styles in order to gain influence and a broad following. Second, the economic activities of the settlement Borough near Alomogordo, NM have been dictated by a chain of individuals that have all appeared to have superhuman intelligence and unusual backgrounds. Complete dossiers on these two individuals are presented at the end of this OCTET CYAN briefing document.

The Wolf Tribe

The second organization to found frontier settlements, the Wolf Tribe is a string of Libertarian extremists and survivalists. While its politics are practically the polar opposite of The New Frontier, its settlements look superficially similar. The Wolf Tribe was founded more recently than The New Frontier, but it already has much greater popularity and over a dozen settlements. While its settlements may all look different from one another they are all internally very homogeneous and often have an overriding culture of xenophobia or theocracy. The tribe is a uniquely American phenomenon, and, somewhat ironically for a group that resembles the Postnational Frontier and requires one to burn their federal ID, their settlements often possess the surprisingly nationalist sentiment that they are the last remaining few "True Americans".

Like The New Frontier, The Wolf Tribe's settlements were only recently made possible by developing technologies. However, unlike them, each settlement has a different wealthy founder and is entirely financed entirely by that founder's stockpile of anything from fiat currency to gold, oil, ammunition, or cryptocurrency.

These communities often advocate strongly for forming militias and overthrowing the government by any means, including violent revolution. This inclination towards violence has attracted the attention of the American secret intelligence agencies. These investigations discovered that, while the tribe's leadership does not include entangled individuals, it does include people that know of and utilize style individuals as muscle, enforcers, and tentative superhuman militia members. Two individuals known by the intelligence community to have OCTET CYAN classified capabilities are presented at the end of this OCTET CYAN briefing document.

Executive Abstract

  • The emerging scientific theory of Orthodynamics proposes explanations for phenomena previously believed to be supernatural. A disinformation and monitoring campaign is currently in place to throttle the pace of this research and prevent leaks of classified information.
  • A consequence of the structure of space is the existence of the Yau, a shadow realm that mirrors normal space.

  • Some people are capable of vanishing from normal space by entering the Yau and passing through solid objects by going deeper into it.

Orthodynamics

All descriptions of Yau and worldline cosmology discussed in this section are based on the current tenets of Orthodynamic Field Theory. Orthodynamics is an emerging theory that attempts to unify quantum mechanics and relativity by proposing the existence 9 orthogonal dimensions of space and a nested three-fold symmetry of all particles. The proposed dimensions include the three measurable dimensions we're familiar with (length, width, and height), the three tiny dimensions curled up at every point of conventional space that cause the Yau phenomenon (θ, ρ, and φ), and three time-like dimensions that result in the worldline phenomenon (line, plane, and radial). Unlike other attempts at unifying theories, orthodynamics makes predictions that closely correspond to classified observations as described in other security clearance keywords.

The field of orthodynamics promises immense societal benefits should it develop to the point of becoming an applied science, however, research into this field runs a high risk of compromising critical secrets classified under the COLD JULY security keyword. In an effort to prevent these leaks, all scholars and institutions engaged in orthodynamics research are closely monitored.

Early experiments in the field have thus far affirmed the theory's predictions, however, to prevent leaking classified information the peer review process of these experiments has been sabotaged and the results have been declared to be not reproducible. Additionally, a disinformation campaign has been instituted that derides the field of orthodynamics as untestable pseudoscience in order to keep growth in the field limited to controllable numbers.

The Yau

The Yau was named as an homage to the manifold structures in turn of the century string theory. At every point in physical space, there is a tiny toroidal-helix shaped manifold composed of the three dimensions θ, ρ, and φ. These manifolds are smaller than any of the particles that make up baryonic matter, and as such all matter floats on top of the manifold and cannot enter or directly interact with anything inside the Yau.

Some exotic particles, namely sterile leptons and achromatic quarks, are capable of of being stable at sizes small enough to fit within the Yau manifold. Particles this small naturally sink down into the Yau and then exist parallel to, but not influenced by, normal physical space. This phenomenon results in the manifestation of a second layer of space just below normal material space.

Anything in the Yau is typically causally isolated from physical space, however certain processes, such as flavor oscillation and spin conduction, can result in information being transferred between physical space and the Yau. Under extreme circumstances the Yau manifold can even be dilated enough for physical matter to enter the Yau. An object that has entered the Yau in this way is no longer visible, tangible or detectable by any means in normal space.

The fundamental forces operate differently in the Yau. Electromagnetism only operates at the distances needed to bind molecules and does not propagate any further. Chromodynamism performs it's normal function of binding atomic nuclei, but also extends a mild repulsive force from physical objects that fades out over several millimeters in the Yau. This repulsion is the cause of the soft and blurry, but still solid, boundaries physical objects project into the Yau. The effects of gravity also fade out over the course of several hundred meters, resulting noticeably weaker gravitation at differing altitudes.

Yau Depth

A being in the Yau can move freely throughout a realm that mirrors normal space. While it can't be detected from normal space, it experiences the shadows of all the matter in normal space. Physical objects appear as blurred silhouettes of their normal selves. The less something moves around in normal space the more solid it's Yau shadow is. For example, a building or landform would appear completely solid and opaque in the Yau while a moving car would be little more than a foggy wake of its motion.

An object's position on the Yau manifold's φ dimension describes how "deep" it is in the Yau. The deeper something gets, the less it is influenced by the shadows of matter in normal space. The depth an object's shadow extends into the Yau is determined by how often it moves. As an object remains still in reference to the local gravity well, it's repulsive residual chromodynamic force extends deeper and deeper into the Yau. In the very shallow Yau everything is completely solid. A little deeper in the Yau objects that are in constant motion are transparent enough that they can be passed through with ease, traffic and crowds no longer present any obstacles but walls and doors do. It is difficult to achieve depths greater than this, but at a medium depth doors and furniture give out, deeper and some newer buildings are gone too, deeper still and nothing is left but the earth. In the extremely deep Yau the landscape itself is nothing more than a blurry shade that can be easily moved through.

Yau Biology

Over the course of Earth's history, life gradually evolved the ability to utilize Yau scale interactions as part of the biological process. While it can't interact directly with the Yau, the nervous system of most vertebrates is nonetheless capable of indirectly influencing the spin of adjacent particles in the shallow Yau. This capability allows the organism to utilize the particles in its Yau shadow as a sort of external computation and sensory organ called the Yau body.

The Yau body originally evolved in fish as a mechanism to the senses by detecting nearby objects and motion. All higher order animals possess a Yau body, and in reptiles, birds, and mammals it evolved to supplement the brain and acts as long term memory storage. In fact, the physical brain has almost no capability for sustaining complex long term memories. In many higher order mammals the Yau body also acts as on-demand supplemental processing power in the brain, however this resource only seems to be accessed during the release of epinephrine.

In humans the Yau body is very highly developed and is also responsible for running a portion of the algorithm for consciousness. Conscious awareness arises from the constant process of comparing the brain's current state with a partial mirror of that state maintained by the Yau body. It's thought that this mirror originally evolved from the virtual state that gets created in dreaming animals as a mechanism for long term memory consolidation and error correction.

Entanglement

The bandwidth of one's brain to Yau body connection is referred to as Entanglement. Most people only possess just enough Entanglement to maintain consciousness. Severing this connection can result in dissociative episodes, loss of consciousness, or amnesia. Additionally, some people have a much greater degree of Entanglement, enough that they have a capability to intentionally control their Yau body that they may not be aware of.

People with awareness and control of their own Entanglement often find uses for it. Using any amount of it above what is needed to remain conscious occupies that portion of bandwidth for maintaining that connection and then recovering from its use over time. This effectively renders that portion unusable until it has recovered. Because of this, people experience Entanglement as a resource, a source of energy that can be used or depleted and regenerates over time.

The Cascade Effect

The primary goal of using Entanglement is to trigger the Cascade Effect.

When a particle exits or transmits information from the Yau, it has the side effect of dilating nearby Yau manifolds and making it much easier for its neighbors to do the same. This can result in a self-sustaining channel of communication between physical space and the Yau known as a cascade. These these two realms are thermodynamically separate, any communication from one of them is effectively the same as creating information in the other. This creation of information from nothing is highly anti-entropic, and, given a large enough cascade, can be strong enough to do raw thermodynamic work. This process of creating work from seemingly nothing is known as the Cascade Effect.

Styles

Those with extremely high degrees of Entanglement can learn to make use of the Cascade Effect. Any specific applied use of this effect is referred to as a Style.

In theory, the applied Cascade Effect could be used to bring any of the three fundamental forces (electromagnetism, chromodynamism, and gravitonertia) to bear on on any point in space that one's Yau body can reach. However, in practice the process of doing something that precise is too difficult to comprehend and control. Instead, styles manifest as Cascade Effects applied in primal ways to capabilities the body already has, namely improving physical and mental attributes. Only those extremely experienced with manipulating cascades and extending their Yau body can exert fine control over forces at a distance.

Cascade Events

Temporary spatially localized events have been observed to occur, naturally or otherwise, that can influence cascade processes. These events vary greatly in their size, intensity, and effects. These events can ease or hinder access to or from the Yau. They can even grant or sever significant amounts of Entanglement.

Spending any time inside any of these events can have unpredictable mental side effects on humans. The most common of these effects is creating dissociative experiences, while in more intense events memory loss or partial to total unconsciousness are common. Prolonged exposure to cascade events can even create permanent changes in one's brain or Yau body. Damage dealt by one in this way can manifest as personality disorders, or conversely, the Yau body can be made more robust, entangled, and aware from prolonged practice in resisting dissociation.

Sublimation

Styles are also capable of causing significant amounts of physical matter to sublime completely into the Yau. This capability is most commonly used to undetectably store objects outside of physical space. However, the true potential of sublimation is realized by shifting one's entire mind and body out of space for the purposes of travel, stealth, clandestine access, or Yau exploration.

While inside the Yau, any physical matter no longer interacts with itself and instead is effectively in suspended animation. A living body in the Yau does not require oxygen, water, or food, but it's brain is also suspended. Conscious awareness is only maintained by a self-aware Yau body, but without a mirror-state running in a physical brain consciousness slowly fades over extended periods of time. Upon returning to normal space, all physical processes resume as if uninterrupted.

Yau Body Entities

When a person's physical body dies, their Yau body dissipates shortly afterward. However, in rare circumstances, certain combinations of highly developed Yau bodies and high levels of dissociation can result in a severed Yau body becoming a separate entity with its own self-awareness and agency. This is most likely to happen in a death, near death, or coma situation that causes the body to shed the Yau body.

Once separated from its original mind a Yau body entity, or Yau spirit, can move freely about the Yau. The amount of awareness and agency that a severed Yau spirit has varies greatly. Some are just simple animalistic repeating loops of instinct lost in the seemingly alien Yauscape while others are practically complete copies of the original person's personality that retain varying amounts of their original memories.

Yau Body Dissociation

A free moving Yau spirit is most commonly created when its original body dies, however, most deaths do not result in a Yau spirit; they are only created when a person dies inside a highly dissociative environment, such during a cascade event. It's also possible, but rare, for powerful dissociative events such as a near death experience or an extremely massive sudden Entanglement gain or loss to cause a Yau body to separate, leaving the original body temporarily weakened but otherwise alive and ready to regenerate a new Yau body over time.

Experiencing a complete dissociation event of this magnitude typically has serious mental side effects, especially if the severed Yau body contained within it a substantial portion of its owner's personality. In general, the greater the amount of Entanglement a person has the more resistant they are to a dissociative event but the more mental side effects they suffer should this resistance fail.

A few hours after total dissociation event, a person's consciousness and mental faculties will have returned, however, there are still sometimes lingering side-effects. These effects most commonly include personality changes (these can be minor or extreme, and frequently involve developments resembling Axis II personality disorders), memory loss (anywhere from a short blackout to total retrograde amnesia), or in extreme cases temporarily diminished skills and mental attributes (the traits most often decreased are Intelligence and Wits). These mental effects are usually temporary, but may last weeks or months as the person acclimates to a new Yau body.

It is possible for a severed Yau spirit to re-associate with its original host body. If this happens any mental side effects the host body may have been suffering will be alleviated over the course of the next few hours. The likelihood of a reunion happening increases in proportion to the amount of awareness imparted into the Yau body. Assuming it wants to, a spirit with enough agency can simply follow its original body until the association becomes possible. This reunion typically becomes possible after the body has completely recovered from the dissociation event and is most easily accomplished during a period of diminished consciousness such as sleep.

Chromaception

Beings in the Yau, including humans that are just visiting via sublimation, have very different senses than what we're familiar with. Sound does not exist at all, light doesn't propagate any meaningful distance, so vision is impossible. The only real sense beings in the Yau have is an omnidirectional object detection capability called chromaception. This sense operates out to several meters by detecting the residual chromodynamic wakes that solid objects possess. These wakes are too diffuse and blurry for chromaception to detect more than the rough shape of something, so texture, color, and complex shapes are not perceivable the Yau.

The lack of sound and light, and the weakness of chromaception make communication between beings in the Yau very difficult. Speech and sign language are impossible. Text is indiscernible, unless each character is larger than about 30 centimeters and has three dimensional depth. A being can make short lived indentations by brushing against an physical object's Yau wake, so slow communication via large scale writing is possible between beings that share a written language. Marks made this way only last for several seconds. Styles and technologies that allow telepathy function normally in the Yau and even between the Yau and physical space.

Yau Entity Interactions

Yau entities are relatively common in the Yau but the vast majority of them are barely conscious and barely detectable. These tend to float aimlessly from place to place or follow repeating behavioral loops. Conscious spirits are rare and not always willing or able to communicate. The degree of awareness in a spirit slowly fades over the course of years until they are reduced to almost nothing.

A being in the Yau is incapable of interacting with an object that is in, or anchored to, physical space. This includes the Yau bodies of living things and any objects that have been placed into the Yau by styles or technology. People that have used the style Sublimation to bring their entire body into the Yau can, however, interact with each other and any objects they have on their person as long as they stay at similar depths.

Worldline Cosmology

The universe we experience is a bigger place than we can imagine, but it is only a small part of a whole. Every moment of our entire universe is arranged in order as a line referred to as a worldline. At one end of our worldline is the beginning of time and at the other, the present moment. As we travel down our worldline we can only experience the present moment. A seemingly infinite number of worldlines radiate outward from the big bang. Every minuscule decision forges a new branch on its worldline, meaning almost any alternate history you can imagine has happened somewhere.

Most worldlines propagate as straight orderly branches, resulting in an overall multiverse with a structure roughly resembling a fractal snowflake. Smaller portions of the multiverse are less stable with worldlines that twist, turn, intertwine, jockey for space, and choke each other off. The angle, shape, and position of a worldline influences the values of the physical constants in the universe within it. The more distant two worldlines are, the more these physical constants governing things like the nature of matter or the speed of light may vary. In fact, relatively few of worldlines are even capable of sustaining solid matter, let alone stars, planets, and intelligent life.

Worldlines that are closer together have much more in common. Two adjacent ones may be indistinguishable except for some minor quirk of history. However, some twists and turns have brought radically dissimilar worldlines together and some worldlines have even become so intertwined that the boundaries between them have become less clear.

Every worldline is composed of two symmetrical parts; the three-dimensional physical space we are familiar with and its parallel Yau space. The physical space portion of each worldline is wholly separate from all others, its contents cannot interact with the contents of other lines. However, the Yau space is less clearly defined and in tightly packed parts of the multiverse it will sometimes blend together with the Yau space of adjacent lines. In such spaces anything capable of entering and traversing the deep Yau could potentially gain access to the adjacent worldlines.

Worldline Incursions

Worldline incursion events can occur when two lines become pushed together tightly enough that the worldline's Yau spaces become enmeshed. When this happens it becomes possible for an object in the Yau of one worldline to enter the Yau of the other one. If this object is capable of exiting the Yau, it can then do so in the other world. During an incursion only the deepest part of the Yau becomes enmeshed with the neighboring worldline and the incursion can only be detected or crossed by the most determined of Yau travelers.

Incursions can be isolated single events or ongoing cycles that cause repeating incursions every time a certain number of years passes. Incursions can also vary in intensity; greater intensity events become more detectable because they allow things to cross from one worldline to the other at increasingly shallow depths in the Yau.

Worldline incursions actually happen quite frequently, but most of them go unnoticed because they are just incursions with nearby worldlines that are so similar that they are practically identical. However, it is possible for more distant, and therefore more different, worldlines to be brought together. Incursions can last any amount of time from minutes to centuries.

Most incursions result in contact with an alternate worldline's version of Earth. While most of the time the two Earths are almost identical it is possible to connect two Earths at different points in history, two earths with different histories, or even two Earths with different evolutionary history populated by entirely different life.

While possible, worldline incursions that result in the contact with more distant worldlines are exceedingly rare. Many variables need to be just right in order for this to happen. A distant worldline may have different physics that don't allow for stars, planets, and life. While physically similar worldlines are attracted to one another, the odds of connecting with one that has a habitable planet near your location are small.

Incursion Entities

A sentient being that enters our worldine from another is known as an incursion entity. The vast majority of these entities are Yau spirits that do not possess any physical form. The deepest parts of the Yau are populated with lost and confused entities from wide variety of truly alien worldlines. Circumstances capable of transferring entities with physical bodies between worldlines are very rare, but have been documented in the past.

Many incursion entities from nearby worldlines are physiologically identical to normal humans, but come from worldlines that are at a different point in history or that have an entirely different history. These entities are referred to as chronological isolates. A human entity brought to a different time is often confused and lacks any way to return to their own worldline.

In the 1960s an incursion that lasted most of the decade occured and resulted in hundreds of entities entering our world. The characteristics of these beings are discussed in documents classified as CIRCUMSTANCE MONO.

During the Ordovician period an incursion estimated to have lasted at least several centuries occured. All evidence for the existence of the entities that accessed our world during this period is discussed in documents classified as VOSTOK BEACHFRONT.

Entities from very distant worldlines may be profoundly alien to us. The possibilities of extremely alien, hostile, and powerful entities gaining access to our worldline is discussed in documents classified as COLD JULY.

Executive Abstract

Resolution Zero

In the wake of the devastation caused by the first World War, the League of Nations enacted the secret Occult Munitions Mandate that forbade nations from utilizing supernatural forces to wage war. This mandate was mostly toothless, unenforced, and not respected by any nation state, but it set the precedent for the United Nations Security Council to adopt Resolution 0 when it was founded many years later.

Resolution 0 is one of the earliest binding resolutions that all member states must adhere to. The resolution outlines rules to prevent poorly understood or seemingly supernational forces from being weaponized, and to protect the populous from global existential threats. It also establishes an extra-national body (the Schedule Enforcement Council, or SEC) to inventory and study the seemingly supernatural, as well as enforcing Resolution 0 in an member nations. The five permanent members of the Security Council (USA, UK, France, Russia, and China) all have exemptions from some of these rules and are allowed to opt out of SEC oversight and operate their own internal intelligence operations instead.

The Occult Munitions Treaty

The first three articles of Resolution 0 establish a treaty that all UN member nations must adhere to. As per the treaty, all member nations must abstain from researching or utilizing any of the seemingly supernatural phenomena listed in the following articles. Additionally, all member nations must adhere to the Schedule and submit to the jurisdiction of the Schedule Enforcement Council as described in the following articles.

It is the official position of Resolution 0 is that there are no truly supernatural phenomena, only poorly understood and incomplete science leading to unpredictable phenomena. Therefore, all poorly understood phenomena must be scheduled controlled.

The Schedule

Articles 4 - 18 of Resolution 0 are frequently revised and amended. These articles establish an inventory of controlled phenomena, artifacts, entities, and technologies that are referred to as the Schedule. The Schedule breaks this list down into seven risk levels, each with different rules regarding what circumstances they may be used or researched under. The list of scheduled phenomena focuses heavily on phenomena that are weaponizable, unpredictable, or present a risk of human rights violations.

Schedule 1: Archived

Schedule 1 includes all technology and knowledge that is considered essential for human society. Information describing how to recreate these technologies and a large body basic scientific and engineering knowledge must be archived at several sites around the globe. These repositories must be accessible using only neolithic era tools and are designed to remain functional for a minimum of a millennium. A dead-hand signal is maintained so that the repositories will begin broadcasting their location in all languages 10 years after any loss of contact with human civilization.

Language, agriculture, power generation, and radio are examples of Schedule 1 concepts.

Schedule 2: Unrestricted

All current technology is considered Schedule 2, and is not restricted. Any well understood technology that proves highly beneficial to bootstrapping a society and can be archived can be reduced to Schedule 1. Recent examples of this occurring are efficient solar panels and solid state water condensers. Alternatively, should any existing technology suddenly become dangerous, it may be rescheduled to a more restrictive level, but this is an unheard of occurrence.

Nuclear fission, chemical rockets, computers, and firearms are examples of Schedule 2 concepts.

Schedule 3: Monitored

Any technology that is well understood theoretically, but has not yet become an applied science is considered Schedule 3. Research into Schedule 3 concepts is permitted, but heavily monitored by Schedule Enforcement Council auditors. All journalism and peer review regarding these topics is carefully controlled so that any progress into a project that proves dangerous can be retracted.

Nuclear fusion, artificial intelligence, and quantum computing are examples of Schedule 3 concepts.

Schedule 4: Internal Use Only

While lower schedule levels typically cover broad technological concepts, Schedule 4 and greater also includes a registry of specific objects. Any instance of an object that could be considered Schedule 4 or greater must be tracked on this registry.

Any use of Schedule 4 objects is permitted only to Schedule Enforcement Council auditors and research staff. Any use or research by civilians is strictly prohibited.

Schedule 4 objects are considered safe and predictable, but their capabilities can not be described by current conventional scientific theories. They must be native to this worldline and only have functions that are well documented and understood according to the predictions of Orthodynamic Field Theory.

Schedule 5: Restricted

Similar to the previous level, Schedule 5 objects must be predictable and well described by Orthodynamics. However, this level includes objects that are not known with certainty to be native to this worldline. All Elusain and Vostok artifacts are Schedule 5.

All Schedule 5 artifacts must be contained inside appropriate Schedule Enforcement Council facilities, and access to them is only granted to SEC research staff.

Schedule 6: Forbidden

Any object or entity with anomalous properties that do not follow a completely understood and predictable pattern are considered Schedule 6. Access to artifacts from this level is strictly forbidden. Research is only permitted by direct mandate from Schedule Enforcement Council leadership.

External SEC liaisons are not allowed any access to or jurisdiction over any artifact, event, or entity that is deemed Schedule 6.

By definition, all sentient entities with anomalous capabilities are considered Schedule 6, including all humans with styles. For human rights reasons, styled humans are somewhat exempt from this level's requirement of absolute containment. All known styled individuals are closely monitored by SEC auditors and forbidden from openly demonstrating their styles. Due to the complex nature of enforcing this policy many individual exemptions and complex relationships with styled individuals and organizations thereof exist.

Schedule 7: Classified

Content deemed Schedule 7 presents a global existential threat. All further information on Schedule 7 is classified under the Cold July keyword.

Schedule Enforcement Council

Articles 19 - 31 of Resolution 0 establish the Schedule Enforcement Council and describe it's organizational structure. The stated mission of the SEC is to promote global safety by preventing organizations, states, and individuals from having access to, and contact with, scheduled phenomena. The bulk of the UN Security Council's funding is devoted to the SEC and this mission.

The SEC has jurisdiction over all UN member nations. This includes the authority to claim all scheduled artifacts, intervene in law enforcement, and even commandeer military forces. This authority is only granted with the understanding that the SEC is required to exercise only the absolute minimum amount of intervention required to fulfill their mission. The Hague arbitrates over any abuse of this authority.

The SEC leadership is located in Geneva, but they have facilities across the globe. The primary goal of the majority of these facilities is to safely contain Schedule violations, however a portion of them are dedicated to research into Scheduled artifacts, Orthodynamics, the Yau, and the cascade effect.

The primary agents of SEC influence are it's auditors. Auditors serve as field agents with two primary goals; to ensure schedule compliance in all member nations and to safely capture and contain all Schedule violations. In order to successfully contain immanent risks, auditors are granted large amounts of unilateral authority to gain access to any organization, intervene in governance and law enforcement, and even requisition and deploy local military forces. Auditors are often accompanied by high-altitude drones, providing them with reconnaissance and rapid missile strike capabilities. This military authority combined with the fact that they are often highly trained hyper-competents or styled individuals makes any auditor a truly dangerous opponent.

External Liaisons

Articles 32 - 34 of Resolution 0 describe the P5 exemptions from the Schedule. This exemption allows the five permanent members of the UN Security Council (USA, UK, France, Russia, and China) a limited ability to opt out of Schedule Enforcement Council oversight by creating their own internal secret intelligence agencies known as SEC External Liaisons. In exchange for taking local schedule enforcement and phenomena containment into their own hands, these liaison agencies allow their parent nations more control over the secrecy and the direction of artifact research within their own borders.

Two major external liaison agencies currently exist and have jurisdictions that have been primarily determined by Cold War era treaties. The United States Liaison Program has jurisdiction over most of the contiguous 48 states, and The Continental Authority has jurisdiction over Russia and China. Additionally, one minor liaison, The Institute of Taxonomy, utilizes the UK and France's P5 exemptions to act as the European Union's shared occult intelligence agency.

The external liaison agencies have 72 hours to contain any schedule violations within their own territory. After this time has elapsed, the SEC takes jurisdiction over the event and steps in to contain it. Additionally, an SEC regulation intended to ensure rapid response to Schedule violations creates a loophole in which these agencies can claim jurisdiction over any Schedule violation event in any UN member nation from the moment it is discovered until the moment Schedule auditors arrive on the scene. This loophole has allowed the liaison agencies to act globally, although international relations may prevent an agency from having access to an event without risking geopolitical consequences. These deadlines have had the effect of increasing secrecy in the liaison agencies in order for them to gain unlimited access to Schedule violation artifacts before the SEC becomes aware of them.

The US Liaison Program

There have long been rumors that the USA's three-letter agencies have divisions tasked with investigating, containing, and covering up supernatural events. In realty, the only institution with a mission resembling this goal is the Department of Defense's International Liaison Program.

The ILP is an organization that's effectiveness is somewhat limited by having multiple competing priorities. Ostensibly, its sole mission to enforce the UN's Schedule and safely contain Schedule violations within the continental United States. However, the DoD's purpose for the program is that it covertly capture Schedule violating artifacts and turn them over to DARPA to research any weaponizable applications they may have. This secondary purpose has greatly enhanced the capabilities of the United States military at the expense of violating Schedule Enforcement Council regulations. A tenuous balance between this weapons development program and similar Russian and Chinese programs within the Continental Authority have prevented the SEC from ending these programs, despite each of these programs being fairly open secrets.

Additionally, the director of the International Liaison Program is appointed by the executive branch of the government rather than being chosen from within the Department of Defense. Throughout history this has resulted in several ILP directors with politicized goals that are somewhat at odds with the DoD's weapons research goals.Often this results an ILP administration that chooses on a case by case basis what artifacts to turn over to DARPA. This has been done in the past by destroying artifacts, turning them over to the SEC, or "losing" them within the bureaucracy of the ILP. This has been done for reasons ranging from enhancing the ILP's internal capabilities to preventing truly terrifying artifacts from getting into the hands of the military.

The ILP also chooses how to handle all styled individuals that it discovers on a case by case basis. Often they are left on their own if the agency deems them a low risk of leaking information or if they are involved in work that advances understanding of the Yau, the cascade effect, orthodynamics or artifact functions. These individuals are always closely monitored to prevent them from leaking information to the general public. These individuals are sometimes even offered positions as ILP field agents or DARPA weapons researchers. On occasions where the discretion of a styled individual cannot be reliably obtained or coerced, these individuals are imprisoned or covertly assassinated.

The ILP has jurisdiction in the lower 48 United States, consequences of the Cold War have kept Alaska, Hawaii, and other US territories under the jurisdiction of the SEC. The ILP is only slightly faster at responding to events than the SEC, so its ability to intervene in international Schedule violation events is limited only to events in which US intelligence has given them a significant head start.

The Elusain

The Elusain are a small population of intelligent beings that ended up on Earth as the result of a massive worldline incursion. This incursion happened repeatedly with varying levels of intensity over the course of the 20th century and reached its peak in the late 1960's. This incursion is also the source of a substantial portion of artifact technology, much of which appears to be Elusain in origin.

There are currently 68 Elusain individuals monitored by the secret intelligence community. The total population of them on Earth is estimated to be between three and four times this number.

Physical Description

Elusain have a roughly humanoid body plan. They are long and slim in build, with heights typically just over two meters, however they're rarely seen in a standing posture up to their full height. They have smooth hairless skin that varies in color from light tans and yellows to dark greenish browns. Some have darker dorsal and lighter ventral countershaded coloration patterns as a vestige of their aquatic heritage. Their long streamlined limbs terminate in highly dextrous hands and feet well adapted for grasping, climbing, and swimming with four long semi-webbed digits opposed by two thumb-like digits. The Elusain have rather minimal facial features, dominated by a pair of large, dark, lidless eyes protected with nictating membranes. Their jawless skulls have no mouth or nose, but rather two large vertical triangular nasal slits protected and partially hidden by folds of skin.

Elusain Biology

The Elusain are evolutionary descendants of smaller semi-aquatic, semi-arboreal creatures well adapted to life in the nutrient-rich swampland. This habitat is so dense with dendritic plant life that brachiation and climbing are the most efficient forms of locomotion, both above and below water. This evolutionary heritage has resulted in a body supported by a fibrous semi-flexible skeleton that is more adept at climbing and swimming than walking or running.

The Elusain are well adapted for, but also heavily dependent on, water. While they do not possess gills and must breath air, they can stay comfortably submerged in oxygenated water for up to three hours. Elusain must completely submerge themselves frequently to avoid injuries due to desiccation. In their native habitat submerging is only required roughly every six hours, but low humidity environments can reduce this time to a single hour.

They are filter feeders with a versatile shared respiratory and digestive system that pulls air or water through their nasal openings into a chest cavity that extracts oxygen from it and then expels it out two openings on their back. During the respiratory process, organs lining the chest cavity filter out plankton-like organisms and digest them. They are also capable of absorbing some simple nutrients transdermally. This system combined with their spongy, diffusion based circulatory system is remarkably efficient and can sustain an Elusain using extremely few resources.

They never truly sleep, but they will spend the majority of their time sessile and marginally conscious between bursts of sudden rapid activity. They can spend extremely long amounts of time sessile using very little energy.

They have no sexes. They all emit sperm-like cells into while resting in water. Individuals enter a breeding phase a few times a year where they become susceptible to sperm implantation in organs on their skin.

Elusain Communication

The nature of the Elusain's senses and anatomy have resulted in them creating languages that are very different from human languages. They have relatively poor vision that is optimized for low-light conditions. They possess hearing that is slightly less acute than ours, but is much better able to discern direction and distance to the source of vibrations. They have an electroreception sense akin to that found in some fish. Both hearing and electroreception operate through the entire surface of their skin. Their most acute sense is a chemo-reception system based out of their facial and dorsal breathing holes. This system is much more developed than our sense of smell and is easily capable of ascertaining directions, distances, and chemical density gradients.

They are also able to excrete a wide variety of terpenes, acids, and other aromatic compounds on demand. This capability combined with their extremely acute chemical senses has produced their unusual languages. The majority of their languages require both chemical excretions as well as their hissing and droning based vocalizations. Their languages are often highly tonal in structure and slow in pace in order to accommodate the time it takes for excrete chemicals to diffuse in the environment. When faster communication is desired, physical touch between communicating individuals is often used to improve latency. This us used most often above water where chemical diffusion is impeded. They have also developed auditory only, chemical only, and gesture only languages, but these are only understood by small minorities for accessibility reasons.

The Elusain are anatomically incapable of human speech and humans are anatomically incapable of understanding the languages most of them know. Theoretically, they could understand spoken and written Earth languages if they learned one, but very few of them that are stranded here have attempted to do so because following an auditory language as fast as ours is uncomfortable for them. Alternatively, human could learn to understand one of their auditory-only or gestural-only languages, but very few of them actually know those languages. This leaves written language as the only likely avenue of communication, but even this has proven difficult.

Elusain Society

They originate from a complex technological society, however their efficient filter-feeding nature allows them to live very comfortably without it. Many of them choose to live natural lives barely above neolithic levels and many of them choose to live in dense post-industrial cities.

The Elusain have Yau bodies similar ours, but they are more predisposed towards being able to develop some Entanglement with them. As a result of this they developed an equivalent of Orthodynamics earlier in their societal development than we did. Consequently, their society is somewhat more technologically advanced than ours and makes extensive use of the Yau and the cascade effect.

Very little is known about Elusain society and culture. This is partially due to the difficulties in communicating with them, however, the primary reason for this that every currently living Elusain on Earth is more than two generations removed from this society. Their life span in ideal circumstances is roughly 40 years, but in their circumstances on Earth it is somewhat lower.

Elusain Origins

It is not entirely clear where the Elusain that are on Earth have come from or why they are here. While we know they do not originate from our worldline, we do not not currently know if they come from another worldline's version of Earth or if they are also from a completely different planet.

Any rudimentary communication that has been achieved with the local Elusain population hasn't revealed much information about their origins. The first Elusain discovered on Earth in the 1940's appeared to be already at least one generation removed from their society and confused about how they got here. It seems that the worldline incursion that brought them here was had enough dissociative Yau-body severing properties that memory loss of their previous lives is practically universal.

The leading theory for the origin of the Elusain on Earth proposes that they were on a generation ship that was more than one Elusain lifetime away from their homeworld when the worldline incursion that brought them here occurred. It is unclear why they were travelling this way, perhaps they were refugees or a military expedition, but the wide prevalence of small arms and other military hardware among the Elusain artifacts on Earth suggests the latter.

Unfortunately, this theory is little more than a guess and is somewhat contradicted by the fact that Elusain possess DNA. Their genes are radically different than anything on Earth, but the fact that these genes are stored in DNA suggests that perhaps they share a common ancestor with current Earth life.

Behavior on Earth

Earth's relatively bright sun, low temperatures, and low humidity is quite inhospitable to them. The few that have survived here did so by avoiding these conditions. The many that did not survive left behind little evidence as their soft low-calcium skeletons rapidly decayed.

Those that survived here most frequently ended up in hiding in one of two environments; secluded swampy rainforests far from human habitation and the steam -filled utility tunnels under large cities and campuses. Those that ended up away from cities almost never leave the water and are all but undetectable. Those that found their way to the utility tunnels were able to use their acute chemoreception senses to stay hidden in the unused nooks and crannies in these concrete mazes.

The Elusain vocabulary of chemicals they can secrete includes some moderately strong acids. They are able to secrete a combination of hydrochloric acid and enzymes that are strong enough to dissolve cement and allow them to dig out concrete aggregate at a rate of about 3 liters per hour with no additional tools. The Elusain living in the city use this ability to slowly excavate hidden unused rooms within the utility tunnels. They then hijack utility pipes to keep these rooms hot and humid and fill bed sized troughs of water to rest in. They then live squalid, sessile lives isolated in these hidden rooms.

Elusain dwelling in these "wet rooms" are all direly malnourished. Their only option to sustain themselves is to sneak out of the utility tunnels and stealing any water soluble food they can find to fill their resting troughs with. Their chemical and vibratory senses make they excellent at detecting and avoiding humans, however modern surveillance makes these nighttime raids an increasingly risky task.

Encountering Elusain

Elusain are excellent at evading detection, especially since they are capable of staying sessile in hiding for long periods of time. Elusain encounters most often occur when someone stumbles across an occupied wet room.

A human discovering one of these rooms would find a dark steamy room that with the sickly sweet stench of a dirty bottle/can recycling center. The beings in this room emerge from water in the darkness with sudden, sharp, stuttering motions and have such alien appearances that reacting to them with anything other than terror is unlikely. Communication with them is all but impossible.

Combatting Elusain

There are several traits that the Elusain possess that make them function differently than humans in high stress situations.

Alien Appearance: The Elusain's appearance and movements are uncanny and frightening to humans. Anyone that encounters an Elusain must succeed on a Guts + Willpower roll with a difficulty of 3 or be stricken with a terror so primal that the only actions they can take are to run as far and fast away as possible. This fear remains until 3 rounds have passed in which no Elusain were seen or heard. Each subsequent unexpected encounter with an Elusain triggers this same roll, but the difficulty decreases by 1 each time.

Gracile Build: Elusain bodies are more fragile than human bodies. They have a wound max that is 1 point lower than a human with the same stats would have.

Vascular Sponge: Elusain vasculature is highly resistant to blood loss. Their endurance score is never reduced by any injury or attack and they are not rendered unconscious by having their upper or lower body disabled.

Metabolic Burst: Elusain are prone to sessility punctuated by limited bursts of lightening quick activity. Each round in which an Elusain takes any action other than resting causes it to lose 1 point of endurance down to a minimum of 1 endurance. Their endurance score regenerates at a rate of 1 point per hour spent resting in nutrient rich water.

Fibrous Skeleton: The soft flesh and fibrous skeleton of an Elusain makes bladed weapons more effective against them. Weapons designed to cut like knives, swords, or axes deal an extra 1+1 damage on Elusain. Weapons primarily designed to pierce, such as spears, fencing swords, and bullets do not gain this damage bonus.

Aquatic Arboreal Heritage: Elusain get 1 bonus success on all Athletics rolls that involve swimming or climbing.

Secretory Arms: Elusain can secrete chemicals that allow them to dissolve, soften, and reshape common calcium based chemicals. When they do not have access modern equipment, Elusain are known to use this ability to fashion armor and weapons. These weapons deal the same damage as any equivalently sized blunt weapon and can just as easily be used by a human. Armor made this way either functions as normal 1 point armor, or heavier 2 point armor with the penalties of 3 point armor. This armor is shaped to perfectly fit its creator and cannot be worn by a human without modification.

Artifacts: Elusain often have access to small arms and equipment capable of artifact functions.

Styles: Elusain are more prone to having styles than humans are. About 25% of the Elusain on Earth have styles.

Playing as Elusain

At the GM's discretion a player may be allowed to play as an Elusain instead of a human character. A GM should only allow this in the if the character's concept adequately explains why they are involved with the rest of their party and they have a plan for overcoming the difficulties of communicating with humans.

Elusain Artifacts

While not all incursion artifacts have an Elusain origin, the broad majority of them do and are discovered in the possession of Elusain on Earth. These artifacts include everything from cultural objects and personal effects to advanced technology, but the most commonly represented items are small pieces of advanced military hardware including small arms, personal armor, and computer components. The poor representation of Elusain cultural artifacts combined with the difficulty of communicating with them has resulted to little insight their society.

A commonly found Elusain artifact is the small computing devices known as casts. The majority of Elusain have casts permanently embedded on their bodies that function as brain computer interfaces. Most casts are little more than a way of connecting to other computers, but some of them possess their own artifact functions and can even augment the physical and mental capabilities of their owner.

Reverse engineering Elusain casts has greatly accelerated the advent of the information age, in particular the miniaturization of computing hardware in the last decades of the 20th century. However, research into larger Elusain computing devices, advanced materials science, and weapon engineering has yielded far fewer advances despite ongoing research.
