Styles

Styles represent the ever improving supernatural powers granted by your by the conscious connection to your Yau Body.

1-3 style points are given out to each character at the end of every session. A particularly long or dramatic session may yield even more points. Style points are the primary way to enhance and customize your abilities.

When you get style points you can either spend them immediately, or save them up for later. If you save them, make sure to write the number of unused style points you have on your sheet.

Style points are spent on upgrading the 9 style classes. Each class relates to an attribute and contains styles that improve that attribute and provide related special abilities and powers. You can spend your style points in any combination of style classes and in any order you want.

Each class has a core style that directly improves the related attribute. Investing in this core style unlocks specialty styles that provide unique benefits as well as granting access to auxiliary style nodes that let you upgrade skills or, for mental attributes, acquire an arcane style.

Using Styles

Stat Bonuses

Many styles, including the core track of each style class, simply improve a stat permanently. After acquiring one of these styles adjust the stat accordingingly, you do not need to do anything special to activate this style.

Passive Abilities

Styles providing a passive ability change the way your character functions permanently. You do not need to activate these and can utilize their benefits freely at any time.

Activated Abilities

Some styles provide abilities that must be activated. These will typically either cause a single action when activated or list a duration during which you gain the benefits of the style after activating it. These styles often require you to spend Speed or Entanglement or use a specific type of action to activate them.

Cascade Events

Some styles grant you the ability to create powerful cascade events. These events will have a duration listed and will always require a point of Entanglement to activate. Unless otherwise specified by the style, creating a cascade event is a combat action costing 8 speed.

Some cascade events will be listed as non-combat cascade events. These events require require a few minutes of intense focus to create. As such they cannot be done in combat.

Buying Styles

You can spend your style points to buy ranks in any of the style classes. Each style class has 3 tiers made up of 3 ranks per tier. Ranks in tier 1 cost 1 style point, tier 2 ranks cost 2 points, and tier 3 ranks cost 3. Buying the first rank in each tier unlocks specialty and auxiliary styles for that tier. Buying ranks in specialty and auxiliary styles have the same cost as the tier that unlocked them. GM's wishing to run a short term faster paced game may opt to have all style tiers cost 1 point per rank.

Once you buy a style your character gets the ability described for it. Many styles give your character additional points in attributes or skills. If you are using paper character sheets, as soon as you buy a style that improves a trait make sure you fill in the related dot for the trait on the front of the sheet and change any rolls or stats that improve because of it.

Core Styles

Each style class has a core style with 9 ranks. Each rank of a core style improves the class's attribute by 1 point and may also unlock specialty and auxiliary styles. If buying a rank in a core style would cause you to increase one of your attributes above the maximum of 9 points, instead draw a circle around one of the filled in dots. While normal attribute dots provide a die to rolls, circled attribute dots instead add an automatic success to rolls. For example, if you have bought enough ranks in a core style to give yourself the equivalent of 11 ranks in the attribute (two ranks above the maximum of 9), you would not add 11 dice to rolls with this attribute, but rather add 7 dice and 2 extra automatic successes.

Specialty Styles

Buying the first rank of each tier of the core style in a class unlocks that tier's specialty style. The unique abilities granted by each specialty style are explained in the following section's description of each style class. Each specialty style has 3 ranks, and buying a rank costs the same number of style points as the tier that it is in.

Auxiliary Nodes & Arcane Styles

Each time you unlock a new specialty style you also unlock a new auxiliary style node. Auxiliary nodes can be used to improve any skill that is related to that style class. You cannot apply more than one auxiliary node to a single skill.

Additionally, the auxiliary nodes in the style classes for the mental attributes (Willpower, Intelligence, and Charisma) may be used to either improve a skill or to acquire an arcane styles that provide powerful supernatural abilities.

Style Classes

Strength

Strength

Each rank improves the attribute Strength.

Rank 1 unlocks Super Strength.
Rank 4 unlocks Power Attack.
Rank 7 unlocks Damage +.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Toughness, Athletics, Manipulation, Unarmed, or Melee.

Tier 1: Super Strength

Rank 1: You can lift twice as much weight as your strength score would normally indicate. You can also ignore the penalties from using large firearms that have the mounted trait. The range of any weapon with the thrown trait is double your strength score.

Rank 2: You can use the dual wield action with any sized weapon (as long as it doesn't have a Bow, Mounted, or any Explosive trait). Double the number of successes you roll when using the Throw action in a grapple.

Rank 3: You can lift three times as much weight as strength score would normally indicate. Your unarmed attacks now deal damage that cannot be resisted by a Stamina roll. Your unarmed attacks also deal double damage to the durability of any artifact with the shield( ) function

Tier 2: Power Attack

Rank 1: You can attempt a power attack as a combat action with a speed cost of 1 greater than attacking normally. When doing so, make a normal basic attack roll, but you may deduct any number of dice from the attack roll and add them to the damage roll.

Rank 2: For each die you deduct from the attack roll of a power attack add an automatic success to the damage roll instead of another die. Additionally, as long as you deduct at least one die from the attack roll you may also increase the speed cost of this attack. Each extra point of speed spent adds another die to the damage roll.

Rank 3: You can spend a point of Entanglement to attempt a knock-out blow against any adjacent target with a current Endurance total that is less than your Strength score. A knock-out blow is a combat action with with a speed cost equal to the target's current Endurance total plus the speed cost of a basic attack with whatever weapon you're using. Roll this like a normal basic attack, if it hits and the damage roll result is greater than the target's current Endurance the the target is instantly reduced to 0 Endurance and knocked unconscious. This attack deals no damage otherwise.

Tier 3: Damage +

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all of your Melee and Unarmed damage rolls and all rolls made while grappling.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional success to all of your Melee and Unarmed damage rolls and all rolls made while grappling.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional success to all of your Melee and Unarmed damage rolls and all rolls made while grappling.

Finesse

Finesse

Each rank improves the attribute Finesse.

Rank 1 unlocks Deft Combat.
Rank 4 unlocks Ambidexterity.
Rank 7 unlocks Melee +.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Stealth, Investigate, Drive, Charm, Manipulate, Unarmed, Melee, Defense, or Ballistics.

Tier 1: Deft Combat

This style improves your stealth attacks, as well as your ability to defend yourself in close combat. Normally stealth attacks simply deal an extra 1+1 damage, with this style they deal more damage, are more precise, and can be done at greater range.

Rank 1: Your stealth attack actions deal 2+1 extra damage. When using one you may opt not make a location roll and instead decide the location that was hit, but doing so prevents you from scoring critical hits.

Rank 2: Your stealth attack actions deal 2+2 extra damage. When using one you may opt not to make an attack roll and instead automatically hit, but doing so prevents you from scoring location roll adjustments.

Additionally, you are now able to defend yourself by expertly blocking and deflecting incoming blows, because of this your melee dodge roll is now either Finesse + Defense or the normal roll of Agility + Defense, whichever is better.

Rank 3: Your stealth attack actions deal 3+3 extra damage and ignore their target’s armor. Additionally, you can use stealth attacks with a ranged weapon anywhere within the weapon's range.

Tier 2: Ambidexterity

Rank 1: When using the Dual Wield action, weapons in your main hand have their normal attack roll instead of a disadvantaged roll. You can quick draw any weapon as a free action with a speed cost of 2 and the speed cost of reloading a weapon is reduced by 1.

Rank 2: When using the Dual Wield action whichever weapon has the lower speed cost has its speed cost reduced to 2. Quick drawing a weapon no longer has a speed cost and the speed cost of reloading a weapon is reduced by 1 again.

Rank 3: When using the Dual Wield action, weapons in your off hand have their normal attack roll instead of a disadvantaged roll and the weapon with the lower speed cost has no speed cost. The speed cost of reloading a weapon is reduced by 1 again.

Additionally, when wielding a melee weapon you can execute a whirlwind strike that targets everyone adjacent to you (regardless of your melee reach). To do so, spend a point of Entanglement and then make a single melee basic attack roll with it's normal speed cost. All targets must defend against this attack result or are they are dealt the attack's damage roll to a single random location.

Tier 3: Melee +

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all melee and unarmed basic attack rolls.

If you are wielding a melee weapon that has a natural speed cost (before any functions or styles are applied to it) of less than 4 and your Finesse is greater than your Strength, use Finesse instead of Strength when rolling damage.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional success to all melee and unarmed basic attack rolls.

If you score multiple location adjustments on any basic attack roll you can sacrifice 3 of the location adjustments to either disarm (the weapon falls to the ground) or knock the target down. You can also sacrifice 6 of you location adjustments on a melee or unarmed basic attack roll to steal the target's weapon. The stolen weapon ends up in your off hand if you are armed and your main hand if you are unarmed. After using location adjustments this way roll damage for the attack normally and if you still have further location adjustments available you can use them normally.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional success to all melee and unarmed basic attack rolls.

Any space within a range of 2 is considered adjacent to you for the purposes of targeting basic attacks and triggering reaction attacks. This extended range stacks with the Reach weapon trait, so with a Reach weapon your melee range extends to 3 spaces (the increased speed cost of using the Reach trait only happens when selecting a target at maximum range).

Perception

Perception

Each rank improves the attribute Perception.

Rank 1 unlocks Chromaception.
Rank 4 unlocks Precision.
Rank 7 unlocks Ranged +.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Alertness, Investigate, Drive, Charm, Firearms, Ballistics, or Initiative.

Tier 1: Chromaception

This style grants enhanced sensory powers by allowing your Yau body to reach out over distances to constantly examine your surroundings. This has two primary benefits. First, your 360 degree situational awareness is greatly enhanced by being able to perceive the residual chromodynamic wake of physical objects and motion. Secondly, by detecting other Yau bodies and their empathic content you gain an awareness what others can perceive.

Without assistance from other styles, this style only allows you to detect objects in physical space. Anything fully in the Yau remains hidden.

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Alertness: Add a bonus of 1 die to all Alertness rolls.
  • Sensorium Detection: You know at all times if anyone can currently see you.
  • Situational Awareness: You are always aware of the rough shape and motion of everything within 3 meters of you, regardless of darkness, blindness, or blocked lines-of-sight. Human beings are instantly recognizable to this sense and cannot hide from you within this range. You can always act in any surprise round of combats initiated from anywhere within this range.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Alertness: Add an additional bonus of 1 die to all Alertness rolls.
  • Detect Artificial Sensorium: You know at all times if you can currently be seen video cameras or otherwise detected by audio recorders and motion sensors.
  • Mid-range Situational Awareness: The detection range of your situational awareness increases to 9 meters.

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Alertness: Add an additional bonus of 1 die to all Alertness rolls.
  • Define Sensorium: You can tell at all times exactly what spaces are currently being observed by humans or devices and their likelihood of being observed in the next round.
  • Long-range Situational Awareness: The detection range of your situational awareness increases to 30 meters.

Tier 2: Precision

Rank 1: You can adjust the location any firearms or ballistics basic attack hits by 1 spot more than normal.

Rank 2: You can adjust the location any firearms or ballistics basic attack hits by 2 spots more than normal.

Rank 3: You can adjust the location any basic attack hits by 3 spots more than normal.

Tier 3: Ranged +

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all of your firearms and ballistics basic attack rolls.

You can use any move action in between Aim action intervals without interrupting it and losing your accumulated successes.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional success to all of your firearms and ballistics basic attack rolls.

Any time you fire a weapon that has a bonus from an Aim action applied to it, you may opt to replace the normal number of damage dice with your Perception score.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional success to all of your firearms and ballistics basic attack rolls.

As a free action with no speed cost, you may spend a point of Entanglement at any time to start an Aim action or roll the next Aim interval immediately.

Stamina

Stamina

Each rank improves the attribute Stamina.

Rank 1 unlocks Resilience.
Rank 4 unlocks Endurance.
Rank 7 unlocks Regeneration.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Toughness, Athletics, Survival, or Defense.

Tier 1: Resilience

Rank 1: Your wound max increases by 1 point.

Rank 2: Your wound max increases by an additional 1 point.

Rank 3: Your wound max increases by an additional 1 point. You can make a Stamina roll to resist a portion of the damage of any physical attack as if it were an unarmed attack; this protection does not apply to damage dealt by Evocation, Chromodynamism, or unarmed attacks improved by the style Super Strength.

Tier 2: Endurance

Rank 1: Adds 3 points to your endurance score.

Rank 2: Adds an additional 3 points to your endurance score.

Rank 3: Adds an additional 3 points to your endurance score. You cannot be stunned, knocked down, tripped, or disarmed.

Tier 3: Regeneration

This style is equivalent to the auxiliary style Regeneration and can fulfill the same prerequisites.

Rank 1: Your wounds stabilize automatically, because of this your Endurance can never drop below 0 and you no longer require medical care to prevent bleeding to death. You automatically heal a point of damage on all locations each hour.

Rank 2: You automatically heal a point of damage on each location every minute.

Rank 3: You automatically heal a point of damage on each bodily location at the end of every round. You are immune to all diseases, drugs, and poisons.

Agility

Agility

Each rank improves the attribute Agility.

Rank 1 unlocks Super Speed.
Rank 4 unlocks Mobility.
Rank 7 unlocks Active Defense.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Toughness, Athletics, Stealth, Drive, Defense, or Initiative.

Tier 1: Super Speed

Rank 1: Adds 3 points to your Speed score.

Rank 2: Adds 3 additional points to your Speed score.

Rank 3: Adds 3 additional points to your Speed score.

Additionally, any time you use a move action to move more than 3 spaces, dodge actions have a speed cost of 1 until your next turn.

Tier 2: Mobility

Rank 1: Using the Traverse action has the same speed cost as moving normally. You do not get your ability to use defensive actions denied by climbing or standing in rough terrain. You can move through the space occupied by any enemy without penalty. You cannot be targeted by reaction attacks.

Rank 2: Double the number of successes you roll as part of a Traverse action. When using a dodge action, you may move 2 spaces for free instead of just 1.

Additionally, anytime you are the target of a melee attack you can spend a point of Entanglement to automatically succeed on a dodge attempt, but you must move two spaces when doing so. You can always use this automatic dodge action against melee attacks, but you can also use it against ranged attacks if there is cover within two spaces of you and you move there as part of the action.

Rank 3: Triple the number of successes you roll as part of a Traverse action. When using the sprint action, the number of squares you can cross is tripled instead of doubled. By using a combination of tumbling, wall-running, and jumping, you can move to any unblocked space as long as there is, at least, 1 space worth of wall or floor somewhere on a path from the start to the destination. This has the same speed cost as moving that distance normally.

Tier 3: Active defense

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all of your dodge rolls.

You can use a Dodge action in between Aim actions without interrupting the aim and losing your accumulated successes.

Rank 2: Add an additional 1 success to all of your dodge rolls.

Any attack that hits you due to a failed dodge roll has all of its location adjustments and critical hit levels removed.

Rank 3: Add an additional 1 success to all of your dodge rolls.

The speed cost for your dodge actions decreases by 1.

Wits

Wits

Each rank improves the attribute Wits.

Rank 1 unlocks Priority.
Rank 4 unlocks Passive Defense.
Rank 7 unlocks Precognition.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Stealth, Investigate, Drive, Survival, Charm, Manipulate, or Defense.

Tier 1: Priority

You can spend a point of Entanglement before rolling initiative to speed up your ability to plan and execute actions. Doing so grants the abilities described in the ranks of this style until the end of combat.

Rank 1: While active, your initiative rolls are 1 die higher than normal. When making an initiative roll, roll it twice. If you get a different number on each roll you will have two separate initiative spots. When you have two initiatives you may take an action when each number comes up during a single turn (if you have enough speed for both actions). If you get the same number on both rolls you only have a single normal initiative score.

Rank 2: While active, your initiative rolls are 2 dice higher than normal. When making an initiative roll, instead of rolling it twice as described in rank 1 you can choose a second initiative spot. You can make this choice after everyone has made their roll and you've seen the initiative order. You may take an action on the number you rolled and another action the number you chose, if you have enough speed for both actions.

Rank 3: While active, your initiative rolls are 3 dice higher than normal. When making an initiative roll, make the extra roll described by rank 1 and choose a number as described by rank 2. This will allow you to take up to three separate actions in a single turn, if you have the speed for them.

Tier 2: Passive Defense

Rank 1: Adds 1 point to your passive defense.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional point to your passive defense.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional point to your passive defense.

When you are hit by an attack that you did not attempt to dodge, you may spend a point of Entanglement as a free action in order to remove all location adjustments and critical hit levels from the attack. You may decide if you wish to do this after you know the results of the attack roll and the location roll.

Tier 3: Precognition

You can spend a point of Entanglement to gain precognitive abilities. Doing so grants you the abilities described the ranks in this style for the rest of the scene.

Rank 1: You can always act in a surprise round, regardless of circumstances. When choosing who to target with an attack, you can ask the GM if that target is going to attempt to dodge the attack or not.

Rank 2: You cannot be targeted by Stealth Attack actions. Once per round you can ask the GM what a single visible enemy's next action will be. This information is accurate unless the combat situation changes radically between when you requested the information and the time the enemy gets to act.

Rank 3: You cannot have your ability to dodge denied by surprise or flanking unless you are being physically restrained. When outside of combat, you have a vague sense of what might happen in the next few minutes, because of this you can ask the GM for hints on what is about to happen. How much information you are given is up to the GM, but the information is accurate.

Willpower

Willpower

Each rank improves the attribute Willpower.

Rank 1 unlocks Entanglement +.
Rank 4 unlocks Persistence.
Rank 7 unlocks Extropy.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Toughness, Guts, or Survival. They can also be used to acquire the arcane styles Abjuration, Evocation, or Sublimation.

Tier 1: Entanglement +

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Entanglement: Add 3 points to your Entanglement score.
  • Yau Body Insulation: When inside a naturally occuring cascade event you can choose whether to completely ignore its effects or to experience it normally. In addition, you cannot be the target of the style Telethesia or the artifact parameter inhibit( ) without your consent. The function noo_interface( ) cannot detect you unless you wish it to.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Entanglement: Add 3 additional points to your Entanglement score.
  • Entangled Insulation: When damaged by an evocation attack or a photonic attack, you may spend a point of Entanglement to cut the damage dealt in half.

    When damaged by by an entropic attack, you may spend a point of Entanglement to reduce it's damage by half.

    When you trigger the entrance effect of a Pneumaplegia mitigation event you can spend a point of Entanglement to ignore any further effects from this event.

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Entanglement: Add 3 additional points to your Entanglement score.
  • Diffuse Insulation: You may apply the benefits of Yau Body Insulation and Entangled Insulation to anyone currently adjacent to you. Any Entanglement spent protecting others this way comes from your Entanglement pool.

Tier 2: Persistence

Well controlled communication between the Yau and normal space allows you to create order where once there was nothing. This style harnesses that anti-entropic property to resist decay, aging, physical damage, and even death itself.

Persisting past your normal lifespan requires near constant two-way communication and manipulation of Yau at all times, because of this you are constantly aware of Yau spirits and they are constantly aware of you. The consequence of this is that as the years and decades go on it becomes increasingly difficult to tell the difference between the real world, the Yau, and the outright hallucinations that will slowly develop.

Rank 1: Your life expectancy is effectively infinite. If you are not killed by physical damage or blood loss you could live healthily forever. Your appearance maintains itself at the age you are most comfortable with.

Rank 2: In the event of your death, only your physical body dies. Your consciousness lives on in your Yau body.

If you have any ranks in Chromodynamism, Electromagnetism, or at least 6 ranks in the core track of the Intelligence style class you may use the non-combat cascade event Create Avatar after the death of your physical body.

If you have any ranks in Sublimation, Telethesia, or at least 6 ranks in the core track of the Charisma style class you may use the non-combat cascade event Create Proxy after the death of your physical body.

Create Avatar
You begin the process of slowly building a new physical body out of raw materials in the environment. If the site you choose to do this in is rich in organic carbon compounds and liquid water you can build a new body in 3 weeks, if not it takes 3 months. In addition to requiring raw materials, this process requires privacy. Anyone discovering your in-progress body will find what appears to be a decaying corpse, if they disturb it you will need to start the process again elsewhere. The ideal location for this process is often buried in the mud under standing water in a secluded location. Your new body has the same attributes as the original one, but you may choose its appearance. Regardless of whether that appearance is beautiful or ugly, each subsequesent body you build is slightly more uncanny and unnerving than the last.

Create Proxy
You begin the process of claiming someone else's physical body as your own by choosing a host body and bonding with it. For one week after starting this process you will be captive in this person's body, experience what it experiences. During this time you have no control over this body and can only communicate with the host or others using Telethesia. During the next week, you can take control over the body while the host sleeps, if your chosen host was in a coma you gain permanent control over it at the start of this week. After these two weeks, you evict the original person's consciousness and now have permanent complete control over the body. Your base physical attributes (Strength, Finesse, Perception, Stamina, Agility, and Wits) are replaced with those of the host body, but your skills and base mental attributes do not change. Regardless of what bodies you choose, each time you take over a new one you give off an increasingly palpable aura of predatory menace. This effect is barely noticeable at first, and will never impact your Charisma, but as your body count accrues you may even start requiring others to make Guts rolls upon meeting you for the first time.

Rank 3: Any time a single bodily location is dealt enough damage to disable or destroy it, you may reduce that damage by one per point of Entanglement you spend down to a minimum equal to how much damage that body part could take before being disabled or destroyed.

Tier 3: Extropy

This style empowers your ability to counter entropy so much that you can create enough raw thermodynamic energy for your body to sustain itself without external resources.

Rank 1: You no longer require oxygen to function comfortably indefinitely. You are also completely immune to the effects of extended exertion, exhaustion, Crisis Sickness, and any diseases or toxins that don't directly corrode flesh.

Rank 2: You no longer need food or water to stay healthy indefinitely. Additionally, if you are capable of using either the Create Avatar or Create Proxy events, you can now use those events before your death in order to prepare multiple bodies that you can spend an hour to begin occupying at any time.

You can only occupy a single proxy at a time and any proxies that you aren't currently occupying regain their original personality, but are now unceasingly loyal to you and will follow your instructions.

If you have multiple avatars prepared you can gain polypresence by occupying more than one at once, but your Intelligence score is split evenly between them (rounding down). Any avatar you stop occupying becomes unusable and begins to decay if you don't exit it carefully in a secluded area with the organic resources to maintain it.

Rank 3: You no longer require sleep to function comfortably indefinitely. If you do not sleep, your Entanglement balancing occurs once per day at the same time. You can now only be killed by letting your upper body reach destroyed status. Other injuries and blood loss cannot kill you, but they can still render you unconscious, which you will recover from at the normal rate.

Intelligence

Intelligence

Each rank improves the attribute Intelligence.

Rank 1 unlocks Analyze Cascade.
Rank 4 unlocks Polymath.
Rank 7 unlocks Episemiotics.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to to improve the skills Hardware, Humanities, Medicine, Science, Software, Survival, or Languages. They can also be used to acquire the arcane styles Chromodynamism, Electromagnetism, or Gravitonertia.

Tier 1: Analyze Cascade

This style increases your comprehension of the fundamental principles underpinning cascade interactions between the Yau and normal space. This knowledge allows you to discern details about cascade events, artifacts, and styles than would otherwise be available.

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Analyze Artifact: By spending an hour closely examining an artifact you can identify and enumerate all of its functions and parameters.
  • Detect Entanglement: You can tell if someone has styles and roughly how much Entanglement they have just by looking at them.
  • Detect Event: You can sense the exact boundaries of any cascade event.
  • Locate Artifact: Whenever an artifact is within a number of meters equal to your Perception score multiplied by the number of ranks you have in this style, you will immediately become aware of its presence and the exact direction to it.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Analyze Event: You can tell the type, nature, intensity, and probable origin of any cascade event by being near it or in it.
  • Detect Aperture: You can see any objects that have been stored in the Yau by the style Sublimation Storage, the function aperture( ), or any other means.
  • Style Comparator: You can tell just by looking at someone whether they have more styles or fewer styles than you.

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Detect Anomaly: You can immediately tell if anything you see originates from, or is manipulated from, somewhere outside of natural physical space. This ability can detect if something came from a different worldline, was assembled by Yau based entity, is controlled Create Proxy, or Telethesia, was created by Create Avatar, or has an otherwise unnatural origin.
  • Eavesdrop: Anytime someone uses the style Ally Communication or the style Telethesia within a kilometer you will immediately become aware of the identities of the participants and, if you wish, the content of the information being transmitted. This ability does not allow you to modify or participate in the any communication that you happen to overhear.
  • Enstylization Quotient: You can tell just by looking at someone how many styles they have and what style class they have the most ranks in.
  • Predict Event: You can predict roughly where and when the next cascade event or worldline incursion within a few kilometers will occur, as well as what the nature of the event will be.

Tier 2: Episemiotics

Your Yau-body can reach out to interface with others, enabling you to understand communication on an empathic level or even to steal memories from others.

Rank 1: You can comprehend, but not speak, read, or write, the spoken language of anyone you can currently see.

Rank 2: You can speak, read, or write any language as fluently as anyone that you can currently see.

Rank 3: You can now induce Excise Memory cascade event.

Excise Memory
Any single fact that a single target knows (or falsely believes) becomes known to you as well. This fact must be simple enough to be articulated in a single sentence. You may spend an additional point of Entanglement when using this event to cause the target to forget this fact after you learn it.

Tier 3: Polymath

Your Yau-body is constantly accessing and archiving the knowledge of those nearby, making you a competent jack-of-all-trades.

Rank 1: If you have zero ranks in any skill that is rolled with Intelligence by default (Hardware, Humanities, Medicine, Science, or Software), instead roll that skill as if you had one rank in it.

Rank 2: If you have zero ranks in any rollable non-combat skill, instead roll that skill as if you had one rank in it.

Rank 3: If you have zero ranks in any rollable skill, instead roll that skill as if you had one rank in it. Additionally, as a non-combat cascade event, you may treat all Intelligence skills (Hardware, Humanities, Medicine, Science, or Software), as if they had two ranks for the rest of the scene.

Charisma

Charisma

Each rank improves the attribute Charisma.

Rank 1 unlocks Induce Cascade.
Rank 4 unlocks Healing.
Rank 7 unlocks a second Combat Healing.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Stealth, Investigate, Guts, Charm, or Manipulate. They can also be used to acquire the arcane styles Pneumaplegia, Schizosomata or Telethesia.

Tier 1: Induce Cascade

This style allows you to create several subtle cascade effects that can be very useful in manipulating those around you or in creating full blown localized cascade events.

Any of these abilities that require an expenditure of Entanglement to activate, also require a minute of concentration and cannot be activated during combat. Some of these effects can have their size and duration increased by spending additional Entanglement. These extensible abilities start with a duration of a minute and a size of a single space that you can see, but each additional point of Entanglement increases the duration by an hour or the size from one space, to a room (or an area up to 9 meters across), to a house (or an area up to 30 meters across). Sizes larger than that cannot be achieved by a single person.

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Cavitation Event: You can spend a point of Entanglement to collapse a small pocket of air into the Yau, producing either a loud phantom sound or flash of light at any point you can see. This cannot be used to create voices, images, or otherwise convey complex information.
  • Induce Dissociation: You can spend a point of Entanglement to cause an area (extensible in size or duration) to temporarily have mentally dissociative properties. Anyone with available points of Entanglement will notice the effect of being in the area, but be otherwise immune to it's effects. Anyone else in the area will be stricken with a sudden intense anxiety and be required to make Guts roll.   • Sever Entanglement: Similar to the Disentangle ability of the previous rank, this ability uses a point of entanglement to cut the connection between the brain and the Yau Body, however this ability severs the connection so thoroughly that everyone in the affected area is immediately rendered unconscious for the entire duration of the effect. Arcane Styles

The auxiliary style nodes of each style class allow you to upgrade skills related to that attribute. Additionally, the auxiliary nodes within the mental style classes of Willpower, Intelligence, and Charisma can be used to acquire the following arcane styles.

Arcane styles are sorted into groups of 3. Each group lists what style class it is available in and a special ability you gain based on the sum of the ranks you have of styles in that group.

Abjuration
Available in Willpower

The following three Abjuration styles, Filter, Retrogradient, and Ward allow you to create protective wards and barriers. If you have ranks in any of these three styles you can induce the following cascade event.

Create Ward
This event creates a translucent barrier of force. The durability, size, and duration of these barriers are all equal to your Willpower score plus the sum of your ranks in all three Abjuration styles. Durability indicates the number of points of damage the barrier can take before it is destroyed, size indicates the maximum width and height in meters your barriers can be, and duration indicates the maximum number of rounds they last for if they are not destroyed or dismissed by you as a free action.

When creating a barrier it appears on a straight line of your chosing that crosses any adjacent space. If this line crosses any occupied spaces, you chose which side of the barrier the occupier is on.

Barriers cannot be crossed by anything and provide full cover until they are destroyed, their duration expires, or you dismiss them. Any attack targeting the barrier or blocked by the barrier deals deals damage to the barrier, reducing it's current durability by the amount of damage done. An attack that reduces durability to 0 destroys the barrier but deals no further damage to anything behind the barrier.

Barriers block things in the Yau as well as physical objects. However, characters with a sum of ranks in Sublimation styles that exceed the current durability of the barrier can phase through it while in the Yau.

The appearance of barriers varies with the individual creating them, and you do have a degree of control over the cosmetic appearance of each one you create. However, unless you have ranks in the style Filter, your barriers are always transparent enough not to hinder vision but still easily visible due to a faint color and luminosity.

Filter

When using the Create Ward event you may add filtering properties to the barrier you create. These filters cannot be used to cause damage; for example, if a blocked object is inside an object that can pass, then both objects are blocked. Additionally, any ranks in this style allow you to choose if your barrier is opaque, translucent, or completely invisible (normally they are transparent, easily visible, and faintly luminous).

Rank 1: When creating your barrier you can target it to a single individual object or entity. The target is either the only thing the barrier blocks, or the only thing that the barrier allows to pass. Things that can pass through the barrier can only do so while moving at speeds slower than 1 space per round. For each additional point of Entanglement spent, you may choose another target.

Rank 2: When creating your barrier you can target it to a specific class of objects, a specific type of entity, or a specific homogenous substance. For example, you could make selections such as, metallic objects, humans, or oxygen. Things allowed through the filter can pass through it at any speed. For each additional point of Entanglement spent, you may choose another target.

Additionally, you can create bio-filters that are capable of removing substances, such as undesirable pathogens or toxins, from a person passing through it. Anyone unaware of the nature of this bio-filter, unwilling to undergo its effects, or moving faster than 1 space per round cannot pass through it.

Rank 3: When creating your barrier you can opt to choose what it filters on a case by case basis. You are aware of anything that comes within a space of the barrier no matter where you are, and if it is about to pass through you can choose whether to allow it or block it. If it returns a second time you can change your previous choice. Creating invisible barriers with this property can allow you discretely monitor an area or set up traps.

Retrogradient

This style allows you to use a defensive action with a speed cost of 1 and an Entanglement cost of 1 to project a defensive field around yourself that lowers the damage of a single incoming attack. Firearms, Ballistics, and Kinetic attacks and any other projectile must move further through this field than others, resulting in greatly reduced damage.

Rank 1: Ranged attack damage is lowered by 1 point. Unarmed and Melee attacks are unaffected.

Rank 2: Ranged attack damage is lowered by 3 points and Melee attack damage is lowered by 1 point. Unarmed attacks are unaffected.

Rank 3: Ranged attack damage is lowered by 6 points, Melee attack damage is lowered by 3 points, and Unarmed attack damage is lowered by 1 point.

Ward

This style improves the duration of barriers created by the Create Ward event and allows you to spend extra Entanglement to increase their size and durability. Additionally, if you have any ranks in this style you may make a barrier that is a sphere with a maximum size equal to the sum of your ranks in all three Abjuration styles. You must be inside, or adjacent to, this sphere when creating it.

Rank 1: The duration of your barriers is now measured in minutes instead of rounds. Each extra point of Entanglement spent when creating a barrier increases it's durability and maximum size by 1.

Rank 2: The duration of your barriers is now measured in hours. Each extra point of Entanglement spent increases on a barrier increases its durability and maximum size by 3.

Rank 3: The duration of your barriers is now measured in days. Each extra point of Entanglement spent increases on a barrier increases its durability and maximum size by your Willpower score.

Rank 3: You can induce the following cascade event.

Invisibility
A willing target you touch (or yourself), becomes completely invisible for a number of minutes equal to your Charisma score plus the sum of your ranks in Schizosomata styles. Any time an invisible target makes a violent motion, including attack or dodge actions, this effect immediately ends.

Shift

This style allows you to change your body, appearance, and even physical attributes.

Rank 1: You permanently become idealized version of yourself. You decide whether that means attractive, youthful, itimidating, or any other appearance you aspire to. At the GMs discretion, this may help or hinder social rolls where appearance is important.

Rank 2: You can induce the following non-combat cascade event.

Shapeshift
Over the course of an hour you slowly and permanently shapeshift into a person of any appearance, build, gender, skin color. For every 10 centimeters or kilograms that your new body differs from your original body, you must spend an additional point of Entanglement to complete the transformation.

Rank 3: When using the Shapeshift event you may also redistribute your starting physical attributes (Strength, Finesse, Perception, Stamina, Agility, and Wits). Any attribute points you have gained from styles cannot be redistributed this way.

Sublimation
Available in Willpower

The following 3 Sublimation styles, Sublimed Storage, Yau Combat, and Yau Traversal allow you to enter the Yau, a parallel realm that mirrors normal space. Any rank in these three styles grants the following non-combat cascade event.

Sublimate Self
This event causes you to enter or exit the Yau, exiting the Yau does not cost Entanglement but still takes a few minutes of concentration. While inside the Yau you cannot interact with, or be detected by, anything in physical space, however the physical world casts shadows into that Yau that impede your movement. You can easily pass through the shadow of any object that has moved within a number of minutes equal to the sum of your ranks in these three Sublimation styles plus your Willpower score, anything that has been stationary for longer blocks your movement.

In the Yau your senses do no operate normally, rendering you blind and deaf. Instead you have an omnidirectional sense called chromaception that can detect the motion and position of anything within a meter of you, however if you have ranks in the style Chromaception this range increases to match the range of the situational awareness granted by that style.

When entering the Yau, you leave your unconscious body behind in the physical world. To exit the Yau, you must return to your body.

Sublimed Storage

This style lets you store physical objects in the Yau and retrieve them later. Retrieving an item puts it where you need it to be for immediate use, for example, a weapon would appear in your hand or a garment would appear already worn on your body.

Rank 1: You can store or retrieve a single object as combat action costing 4 speed. You can store as many items in the Yau as you can fit in 1 cubic meter of space.

Rank 2: You can store or retrieve 1-3 objects at once as a combat action costing 1 speed. You can store as many items in the Yau as you can fit in 2 cubic meters of space.

Rank 3: You can store or retrieve any number of objects as a free action with no speed cost. You can store as many items in the Yau as you can fit in 3 cubic meters of space.

Yau Combat

This style allows you to bring your physical body with you when entering the Yau and improves the tactical capabilities of subliming your body.

Rank 1: You Sublimate Self events are now a combat actions costing 6 speed. When entering the Yau your body vanishes from physical space and reappears when you exit. You may exit the Yau any place you can access while in it.

Rank 2: You can gain the ability to induce the following cascade event.

Blink
Teleport by entering the Yau and instantly exiting it anywhere within a range equal to 3 plus your Willpower score. You cannot teleport through walls or obstacles. The speed cost of this event is equal to half (round up) of the amount of Speed it would have taken to reach that location normally.

Rank 3: Your Blink events can teleport you anywhere within a range equal to 6 plus double your Willpower score. You can teleport through any obstacle that your ranks in Sublimation would allow you to pass through while in the Yau.

Yau Traversal

This style allows you to pass through more physical objects by entering deeper reaches of the Yau. Without this style, you can pass through physical objects that have moved within a specified number of minutes, with it this same number is measured in greater units of time.

Rank 1: The time an object must have moved more recently than for you to pass through it while in the Yau is now measured in hours.

Rank 2: The time an object must have moved more recently than for you to pass through it while in the Yau is now measured in days. Additionally, when you are this deep in the Yau you can effortlessly cross physical space at rates up to 1000 kilometers per hour.

Rank 3: The time an object must have moved more recently than for you to pass through it while in the Yau is now measured in years. Additonally, when you are this deep in the Yau you can ignore gravity and travel anywhere on the planet within an hour.

Telethesia
Available in Charisma

The following 3 Telethesia styles, Clairvoyance, Suggestion, and Telepathy sense things at a distance as well as read or influence minds. The sum of your ranks in these styles increases the distance of ally communication.

Addtionally, you can spend a point of Entanglement as a free action to enable telepathic communication between a number of willing participants equal to the sum of your ranks in Telethesia styles. Choose the participants when activating this style, these participants stay in telepathic contact with you and each other for the rest of the scene. Different participants cannot be chosen without activating the effect again. Telepathic communication occurs at the same speed and bandwidth of normal speech. Telepathic communication is undetectable by all means other than the Eavesdrop ability granted by the style Analyze Cascade. This channel of communication remains open for the rest of the scene.

The sum of your ranks in Telethesia styles determines the how close someone must be to you to include them in this communication.

1: Within 9 spaces.

2: Within half a kilometer.

3: Within 3 kilometers.

4: Within 30 kilometers.

5: Within 300 kilometers.

6: On the same continent.

7: In the same hemisphere.

8: On the same planet.

9: Anywhere in the solar system. Speed of light delays apply.

Clairvoyance

As a non-combat cascade event, you can sense what happens at distant locales regardless of any obstacles in the way. The range within which you can sense this way is equivalent to the range that the sum of your ranks in all Telethesia styles allows you to communicate across.

Rank 1: You can scry on any location within range, but you must have previously seen this location normally at some point. You only gain vision of what is occuring at this location for the next minute.

Rank 2: You can scry on any location within range, regardless of whether you have seen it before. You gain all senses at this location for the next hour.

Rank 3: You can scry on any location within range and sense what occured there at any single point in the past within a number of years equal to your Charisma score. You gain all senses of what occurred at this location and may skim through the times you can see in search of specific events.

Suggestion

As a non-combat cascade event, you can attempt to exert influence over the minds of a single target. The degree of influence you can gain is determined by your ranks in this style.

The target rolls Willpower + Guts against your Charisma + Guts to attempt to resist your influce. If the target has any Entanglement add their current number of available Entanglement points to the number of dice in their resistance roll. The results of this roll indicate how much influence you gain over the target.

Rank 1: You can attempt to stimulate a target’s brain to briefly release a single specific neurotransmitter. This will allow you to create in the target a basic emotion or vague sensation. High degrees of success over your target can make this feeling extremely intense or evoke feelings that radically differ from the target's current state.

Rank 2: You can attempt to instill a suggestion in the target that they will have a hard time resisting. Tying the target's roll may only allow simple suggestion that they will not put much effort into, while greatly beating the target may allow you to issue complex commands and orders that the target is irresistably compelled to follow. The more these suggestions differ from the target's desires, the more successes you'll need to beat them by.

Rank 3: You can attempt to implant false memories into the target. The more complex and implausible these memories are, the more successes you'll need to beat the target by. Just tying their roll may only lead to instilling them with a vague deja vu, while beating them by several succcesses may allow you to completely rewrite a phase of their life.

Telepathy

As a non-combat cascade event, you can attempt to discern the thoughts and memories of others. The kinds of information you can get access to are determined by your ranks in this style.

The target rolls Willpower + Guts against your Charisma + Guts to attempt to resist you. If the target has any Entanglement add their current number of available Entanglement points to the number of dice in their resistance roll.

Rank 1: If you tie or beat the target's resistance roll you become aware of the target’s overall state of mind, general emotional state, and stress level for the rest of the scene.

Rank 2: If you tie or beat the target's resistance roll you become aware of the target's entire internal monologue for the rest of the scene.

Rank 3: The more successes you beat the target's resistance roll, the more deeply you can probe into their memory. Recent memories are easy to access, while deep dark secrets will require beating the target by multiple successes.

Optional Rule: Outlook Styles

This section describes optional rules. Ask your GM if your group is using outlook styles before adding an outlook or one of these styles to your character.

You can select an outlook for your character that represents their how they understand and experience their Entanglement and styles. Most characters are less than certain about how these things work. These charactes are represented by the neutral outlook and have access to the styles described in the rest of this chapter. If you instead choose one of the other three outlooks for your character, your character will have two of the specialty styles they have access to replaced with alternative styles.

Most of the descriptions in this rulebook are from the point of view of how the Emipirical Theory outlook understands the world. If you choose another outlook, you may come up with your own alternative explanations and terminology for how Styles and Entanglement work.

Neutral Outlook

You are looking for the answers to life's mysteries, just like everyone else. Most characters have this outlook, and are unsure or unopinionated about what the source of their powers is.

Empirical Theory

The universe is fundamentally knowable.

Through observation and falsifiable predictions, even the most mysterious phenomena can be understood. Your powers are best described as manipulating your Yau-body entanglement to induce a cascade of free energy as predicted by Orthodynamic Field Theory.

If you choose this outlook, the Intelligence specialty styles Episemiotics and Polymath are replaced by the alternative styles Embed Function and Embed Cast.

Tier 2: Embed Function

This style allows you to create items with artifact functions. In order to acquire this style you must have had the chance to thoroughly examine at least one item with artifact functions.

Creating an artifact is an extended action with an interval of a week and a roll of Intelligence + Hardware. When creating an item, each function rank you wish to add requires 6 successes to be accumulated. Each function rank you wish to add to an item requires about $1000 worth or raw materials.

Adding functions to existing artifacts can be done at the same rate of 6 successes and $1000 per rank. Removing functions can be done for just 1 success per rank. However, if you are not the original creator of the artifact, the first time you try to change its functions you must roll 3 successes to familiarize yourself with the item before changing it.

You cannot add more than a single primary function to an item or create artifacts with the Cast form factor or without the style Embed Cast.

Rank 1: You can create items with a primary function of any rank.

Rank 2: You can create items with a single parameter of any rank.

Rank 3: You can create items with all three parameters that their primary function can host.

Tier 3: Embed Cast

This style allows you to create artifacts that can interface with the human body smoothly enough to have the Cast form factor. It also increases the number of primary functions you can add to any artifact. If you do not also have ranks in Embed Function, you can only make items with the first rank of their primary function. If you do have ranks in Embed Function, any primary function can have ranks and parameters according to your rank in that style.

This style also allows you to safely remove casts from yourself or other cast hosts that are either willing or unconscious. Removing a cast takes few hours to accomplish.

Rank 1: You can create casts with a single primary function.

Rank 2: You can create casts or other artifacts with up to two primary functions.

Rank 3: You can create casts or other artifacts with up to three primary functions.

Crisis Paradigm

The natural world arises from balance.

Balance between extremes defines the emergent phenomena behind all of nature and humanity. Your Entanglement is best understood as temporary imbalance in nature, by carefully releasing the tension of this small crisis of balance your styles become manifest.

If you choose this outlook, the Willpower specialty styles Persistence and Extropy are replaced by the alternative styles Reverberation and Familiar.

Tier 2: Reverberation

A meditation practice taught by those that view Entanglement as a temporary imbalance in nature, Reverberation allows you to store a large pool of temporary Entanglement points.

To use this style you must maintain concentration. Taking any damage, making any rolls that involve the skill Guts or any combat skill, or otherwise being severely distracted breaks this concentration. Once this concentration ends, any unused temporary Entanglement gained while concentrating fades away after a number of minutes equal to your Charisma score.

While concentrating, and for the first minute afterwards, you are immune to any discomfort and to any penalties or damage from crisis sickness that high levels of Entanglement may cause. After this minute, if your Entanglement is still boosted it effects you normally.

Rank 1: For each minute spent concentrating on this style, gain one temporary point of Entanglement. The maximum amount of temporary Entanglement you can store this way is three. You cannot spend any Entanglement while concentrating.

Rank 2: For each minute spent concentrating on this style, gain two temporary points of Entanglement. The maximum amount of temporary Entanglement you can store this way is equal to your normal Entanglement maximum. You can now spend Entanglement while concentrating, but it comes from your original Entanglement total, not the temporary points.

Rank 3: For each minute spent concentrating on this style, gain three temporary points of Entanglement. The maximum amount of temporary Entanglement you can store this way is equal to either your normal Entanglement maximum or your current Entanglement total, whichever is higher. If you spend Entanglement while concentrating you may choose if it comes from your original current total or from the temporary pool.

Tier 3: Embed Cast

Detached fragments of Yau-bodies roam the Yau. These liminally conscious entities, sometimes referred to as Yau spirits, are barely detectable most of the time. With this style you become able to reliably detect and interact with these entities.

Yau spirits vary widely from sub-animal to super-human levels of awareness or Intelligence and from undetectable to unavoidable. They may be completely ignorant of, or extremely aware of, events in the physical world. They also may have differing methods of communication, anything from direct telepathy, spoken word, manifested writing, or editing digital content are possible.

Rank 1: Regardless of the nature of any entity in the Yau you can always detect it and find a way to communicate with it.

Rank 2: You can induce the Coalesce Familiar non-combat cascade event.

Coalesce Familiar
By merging fragments of your own psyche and other minor Yau spirits you create a new entity in the Yau known as a familiar. This familiar is loyal to you and exists for as long as you have at least one point of Entanglement. It can travel through the Yau independently of you at high speeds and communicate back to you telepathically at any distance. Familiars can observe any event in the Yau or in physical space, but otherwise cannot influence, or be detected from, physical space.

Rank 3: Familiars you create can manifest enough influence on physical space to slowly move any unattended object that weighs less than 3 kilograms. You can also direct them to make a proxy out of any non-human animal you encounter that has a weak Yau-body. This includes all fish, reptiles, birds, as well as newborn mammals. Your familiar has complete control of this animal for as long as you have at least three points of Entanglement.

Consensus Praxis

Belief shapes all of reality.

The world is little more than the ontological consensus of the beliefs of its inhabitants. Your powers arise from the knowledge that this consensus is mutable and that your will can briefly overcome it as long as you truly believe it can.

If you choose this outlook, the Charisma specialty styles Healing and Combat Healing are replaced by the alternative styles Influence and Illusion.

Tier 2: Influence

One of the earliest purposes the Yau-body evolved to fill was a primitive way to identify friends and foes within one's own species. This style allows you to hijack the vestiges of this system in humans in order to gain their trust.

Rank 1: You can induce the Charm Person non-combat cascade event.

Charm Person
A single person you can see must succeed on a Willpower + Guts roll with a difficulty equal to your Charisma score or their opinion of you will be changed to that of a stranger or an acquaintance for the rest of the scene. If you choose stranger, they will not recognize you no matter how familiar they were previously. If you choose acquaintance, they will regard you as someone they have met before and have generally positive feelings towards. If you spend more than a minute interacting with a charmed target they will know you manipulated them in some way.

Rank 2: You can now use the a successful Charm Person event to cause the target to regard you as a trusted friend. They will still use their common sense when it comes to detecting deceit or deciding to follow through on a request, but they will give you the benefit of the doubt and default to trusting you until you give them a reason not to. If you spend more than three minutes interacting with a charmed target they will know you manipulated them in some way.

Rank 3: You can now use Charm Person events in combat. Successfully doing so causes the target to become an ally that will not attack you, and with enough convincing may even turn on their previous allies. This effect ends a minute after combat ceases, after which the target becomes aware that they were manipulated.

Using Charm Person events to create friends, strangers, or acquaintances outside of combat will no longer result in the target knowing they were manipulated after any amount of time.

Tier 3: Illusion

This style is the most effective way to manipulate the consensus on what the nature of reality is without completely violating the laws of physics.

Illusions created by this style are entirely in the mind of those viewing them, they have no manifestation in physical space.

Rank 1: You can induce the Create Illusion cascade event.

Create Illusion
A single person now begins perceiving an illusion at a point you choose. With one rank in this style, this illusion must be an unmoving two-dimensional visual image no greater than one meter in size. This illusion is flawlessly convincing, although if it suddenly appears in someone's line of sight they will likely be suspicious of it. However, the two-dimensional nature of this image means that as soon as anyone witnessing it moves at least one space they will immediately see the true illusory nature of it. Illusions you create last for a number of minutes equal to your Charisma score.

Rank 2: Your Create Illusion events can now be up to three meters in size and are visible to a number of people equal to or less than your Charisma score. They are now three-dimensional images that are convincing even after changing viewing angles, however they still do not stand up to any physical inspection. They can include basic animation that lasts no longer than a few seconds before repeating or stopping. They can also include a single non-verbal sound that lasts no longer than a few seconds before repeating or stopping. They can now last for the duration of the scene.

Additionally, you can now induce the Suppress Sensorium cascade event.

Suppress Sensorium
At any point you can see, create a 9 meter sphere that blocks all vision, including yours. Anyone looking at it from the outside sees only an opaque sphere, anyone inside is blinded

Rank 3: Your Create Illusion events can now be up to nine meters in size and are perceivable to anyone with line of sight to them. They can fool all senses, including touch and proprioception, well enough that they remain convincing even after a physical inspection and can block movement. They can include any complex animation or audio and last any amount of time up to an hour after you leave the area.

Additionally, your Suppress Sensorium events can now block all senses. Nothing inside the sphere can be detected and everyone completely loses all of their senses while inside it. However, you are now immune to this event and all of your senses are unaffected by it.
