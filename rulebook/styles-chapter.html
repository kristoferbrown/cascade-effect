Styles

Styles represent the ever improving supernatural powers granted by your by the conscious connection to your Yau Body.

1-3 style points are given out to each character at the end of every session. A particularly long or dramatic session may yield even more points. Style points are the primary way to enhance and customize your abilities.

When you get style points you can either spend them immediately, or save them up for later. If you save them, make sure to write the number of unused style points you have on your sheet.

Style points are spent on upgrading the 9 style classes. Each class relates to an attribute and contains styles that improve that attribute and provide related special abilities and powers. You can spend your style points in any combination of style classes and in any order you want.

Each class has a core style that directly improves the related attribute. Investing in this core style unlocks specialty styles that provide unique benefits as well as granting access to auxiliary style nodes that let you upgrade skills or acquire other benefits. In addition to the 9 style classes, there are also arcane styles that are unlocked by certain specialty styles.

Buying Styles

You can spend your style points to buy ranks in any of the style classes. Each style class has 3 tiers made up of 3 ranks per tier. Ranks in tier 1 cost 1 style point, tier 2 ranks cost 2 points, and tier 3 ranks cost 3. Buying the first rank in each tier unlocks specialty and auxiliary styles for that tier. Buying ranks in specialty and auxiliary styles have the same cost as the tier that unlocked them. GM's wishing to run a short term faster paced game may opt to have all style tiers cost 1 point per rank.

Once you buy a style your character gets the ability described for it. Many styles give your character additional points in attributes or skills. If you are using paper character sheets, as soon as you buy a style that improves a trait make sure you fill in the related dot for the trait on the front of the sheet and change any rolls or stats that improve because of it.

Core Styles

Each style class has a core style with 9 ranks. Each rank of a core style improves the class's attribute by 1 point and may also unlock specialty and auxiliary styles. If buying a rank in a core style would cause you to increase one of your attributes above the maximum of 9 points, instead draw a circle around one of the filled in dots. While normal attribute dots provide a die to rolls, circled attribute dots instead add an automatic success to rolls. For example, if you have bought enough ranks in a core style to give yourself the equivalent of 11 ranks in the attribute (two ranks above the maximum of 9), you would not add 11 dice to rolls with this attribute, but rather add 7 dice and 2 extra automatic successes.

Specialty Styles

Buying the first rank of each tier of the core style in a class unlocks that tier's specialty style. The unique abilities granted by each specialty style are explained in the following section's description of each style class. Each specialty style has 3 ranks, and buying a rank costs the same number of style points as the tier that it is in.

Auxiliary Styles

Each time you unlock a new specialty style you also unlock a new auxiliary style node. Auxiliary nodes can be used to either improve a skill that is related to that style class or to acquire an auxiliary style. The next section describing each style class contains a list of related skills for each class, auxiliary nodes in that class can only be used to upgrade skills in that list. You cannot apply more than one auxiliary node to a single skill. Auxiliary styles are described in their own section later in this chapter.

Arcane Styles

Arcane styles provide overtly supernatural effects and are only available to acquire after being unlocked by the second and third tier specialty styles in the Willpower, Intelligence, or Charisma style classes. Each rank you acquire of these specialty styles makes one arcane tier of a chosen arcane style available for purchase. Each arcane tier composed of three ranks, and buying a rank costs a number of style points equal to the number of the arcane tier that the rank is in, regardless of what tier the specialty style that unlocked the arcane style was.

Style Classes

Strength

Strength

Each rank improves the attribute Strength.

Rank 1 unlocks Super Strength.
Rank 4 unlocks Power Attack.
Rank 7 unlocks Damage +.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the style Focus Strike, or to improve the skills Toughness, Athletics, Manipulation, Unarmed, or Melee.

Tier 1: Super Strength

Rank 1: You can lift twice as much weight as your strength score would normally indicate. You can also ignore the penalties from using large firearms that have the mounted trait. The range of any weapon with the thrown trait is double your strength score.

Rank 2: You can use the dual wield action with any sized weapon (as long as it doesn't have a Bow, Mounted, or any Explosive trait). Double the number of successes you roll when using the Throw action in a grapple.

Rank 3: You can lift three times as much weight as strength score would normally indicate. Your unarmed attacks now deal damage that cannot be resisted by a Stamina roll. Your unarmed attacks also deal double damage to the durability of any artifact with the shield( ) function

Tier 2: Power Attack

Rank 1: You can attempt a power attack as a combat action with a speed cost of 1 greater than attacking normally. When doing so, make a normal basic attack roll, but you may deduct any number of dice from the attack roll and add them to the damage roll.

Rank 2: For each die you deduct from the attack roll of a power attack add an automatic success to the damage roll instead of another die. Additionally, as long as you deduct at least one die from the attack roll you may also increase the speed cost of this attack. Each extra point of speed spent adds another die to the damage roll.

Rank 3: You can spend a point of Entanglement to attempt a knock-out blow against any adjacent target with a current Endurance total that is less than your Strength score. A knock-out blow is a combat action with with a speed cost equal to the target's current Endurance total plus the speed cost of a basic attack with whatever weapon you're using. Roll this like a normal basic attack, if it hits and the damage roll result is greater than the target's current Endurance the the target is instantly reduced to 0 Endurance and knocked unconscious. This attack deals no damage otherwise.

Tier 3: Damage +

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all of your Melee and Unarmed damage rolls and all rolls made while grappling.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional success to all of your Melee and Unarmed damage rolls and all rolls made while grappling.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional success to all of your Melee and Unarmed damage rolls and all rolls made while grappling.

Finesse

Finesse

Each rank improves the attribute Finesse.

Rank 1 unlocks Zone of Denial.
Rank 4 unlocks Ambidexterity.
Rank 7 unlocks Melee +.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to improve the skills Stealth, Investigate, Drive, Charm, Manipulate, Unarmed, Melee, Defense, or Ballistics. There are no styles that fit in Finesse auxiliary nodes.

Tier 1: Zone of Denial

Rank 1: Any time you are wielding a melee weapon you can spend a point of Entanglement as a free action to designate any 3 spaces that are all adjacent to you as a zone of denial. The zone remains active in each space for as long you remain adjacent to that space. If you move, any active space that you are still adjacent to remains active and any active space you are no longer adjacent to stops being active. While the zone is active, you may make a normal basic melee attack as a free action with a speed cost of 1 the first time any target triggers the zone in a turn (once per target each turn). This attack happens just after the action that triggered it and does not use up your turn.

Targets trigger the zone any time they make a move action that results in them entering or leaving the zone. Entering or leaving the space as a part of any action other than a move action does not trigger the zone. Enemies can tell from your body language that there will be consequences for triggering the zone.

Enemies with the Agility style Mobility never trigger your zone, but they are denied the ability to pass through your space.

Rank 2: You are able to defend yourself by expertly blocking and deflecting incoming blows, because of this your melee dodge roll is now either Finesse + Defense or the normal roll of Agility + Defense, whichever is better.

Rank 3: Where creating a zone of denial, all adjacent spaces are part of the zone. Free action attacks granted by this style no longer have any speed cost and they now happen before the action that triggered them instead of after.

Anytime an opponent starts a turn inside your zone of denial, they must either target you with a basic melee attack action or leave the zone as part of the single space movement granted by an action. If they take any other action after starting a turn in your zone they trigger the zone and can be dealt a free attack before they take their action.

Tier 2: Ambidexterity

Rank 1: When using the Dual Wield action, weapons in your main hand have their normal attack roll instead of a disadvantaged roll. You can quick draw any weapon as a free action with a speed cost of 2 and the speed cost of reloading a weapon is reduced by 1.

Rank 2: When using the Dual Wield action whichever weapon has the lower speed cost has its speed cost reduced to 2. Quick drawing a weapon no longer has a speed cost and the speed cost of reloading a weapon is reduced by 1 again.

Rank 3: When using the Dual Wield action, weapons in your off hand have their normal attack roll instead of a disadvantaged roll and the weapon with the lower speed cost has no speed cost. The speed cost of reloading a weapon is reduced by 1 again.

Additionally, if you also have ranks in Zone of Denial, when wielding a melee weapon you can execute a whirlwind strike that targets everyone that is in a space that you could designate as a zone of denial (meaning any 3 adjacent spaces at rank 1, and all adjacent spaces at rank 3). To do so, spend a point of Entanglement, choose your targets, and then make a single melee basic attack roll with it's normal speed cost. All targets must defend against this attack result or are they are dealt the attack's damage roll to a single random location.

Tier 3: Melee +

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all melee and unarmed basic attack rolls.

If you are wielding a melee weapon that has a natural speed cost (before any functions or styles are applied to it) of less than 4 and your Finesse is greater than your Strength, use Finesse instead of Strength when rolling damage.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional success to all melee and unarmed basic attack rolls.

If you score multiple location adjustments on any basic attack roll you can sacrifice 3 of the location adjustments to either disarm (the weapon falls to the ground) or knock the target down. You can also sacrifice 6 of you location adjustments on a melee or unarmed basic attack roll to steal the target's weapon. The stolen weapon ends up in your off hand if you are armed and your main hand if you are unarmed. After using location adjustments this way roll damage for the attack normally and if you still have further location adjustments available you can use them normally.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional success to all melee and unarmed basic attack rolls.

Any space within a range of 2 is considered adjacent to you for the purposes of targeting basic attacks and activating Zone of Denial. This extended range stacks with the Reach weapon trait, so with a Reach weapon your melee range extends to 3 spaces (the increased speed cost of using the Reach trait only happens when selecting a target at maximum range).

Perception

Perception

Each rank improves the attribute Perception.

Rank 1 unlocks Chromaception.
Rank 4 unlocks Precision.
Rank 7 unlocks Ranged +.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the style Occult Spectra, or to improve the skills Alertness, Investigate, Drive, Charm, Firearms, Ballistics, or Initiative.

Tier 1: Chromaception

This style grants enhanced sensory powers by allowing your Yau body to reach out over distances to constantly examine your surroundings. This has two primary benefits. First, your 360 degree situational awareness is greatly enhanced by being able to perceive the residual chromodynamic wake of physical objects and motion. Secondly, by detecting other Yau bodies and their empathic content you gain an awareness what others can perceive.

Without assistance from other styles, this style only allows you to detect objects in physical space. Anything fully in the Yau remains hidden.

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Alertness: Add a bonus of 1 die to all Alertness rolls.
  • Sensorium Detection: You know at all times if anyone can currently see you.
  • Situational Awareness: You are always aware of the rough shape and motion of everything within 3 meters of you, regardless of darkness, blindness, or blocked lines-of-sight. Human beings are instantly recognizable to this sense and cannot hide from you within this range. You can always act in any surprise round of combats initiated from anywhere within this range.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Alertness: Add an additional bonus of 1 die to all Alertness rolls.
  • Detect Artificial Sensorium: You know at all times if you can currently be seen video cameras or otherwise detected by audio recorders and motion sensors.
  • Mid-range Situational Awareness: The detection range of your situational awareness increases to 9 meters.

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Alertness: Add an additional bonus of 1 die to all Alertness rolls.
  • Define Sensorium: You can tell at all times exactly what spaces are currently being observed by humans or devices and their likelihood of being observed in the next round.
  • Long-range Situational Awareness: The detection range of your situational awareness increases to 30 meters.

Tier 2: Precision

Rank 1: You can adjust the location any firearms or ballistics basic attack hits by 1 spot more than normal.

Rank 2: You can adjust the location any firearms or ballistics basic attack hits by 2 spots more than normal.

Rank 3: You can adjust the location any basic attack hits by 3 spots more than normal.

Tier 3: Ranged +

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all of your firearms and ballistics basic attack rolls.

You can use any move action in between Aim action intervals without interrupting it and losing your accumulated successes.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional success to all of your firearms and ballistics basic attack rolls.

Any time you fire a weapon that has a bonus from an Aim action applied to it, you may opt to replace the normal number of damage dice with your Perception score.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional success to all of your firearms and ballistics basic attack rolls.

As a free action with no speed cost, you may spend a point of Entanglement at any time to start an Aim action or roll the next Aim interval immediately.

Stamina

Stamina

Each rank improves the attribute Stamina.

Rank 1 unlocks Resilience.
Rank 4 unlocks Endurance.
Rank 7 unlocks Regeneration.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the styles Diffuse Regeneration or Retrogradient, or to improve the skills Toughness, Athletics, Survival, or Defense.

Tier 1: Resilience

Rank 1: Your wound max increases by 1 point.

Rank 2: Your wound max increases by an additional 1 point.

Rank 3: Your wound max increases by an additional 1 point. You can make a Stamina roll to resist a portion of the damage of any physical attack as if it were an unarmed attack; this protection does not apply to damage dealt by Evocation, Chromodynamism, or unarmed attacks improved by the style Super Strength.

Tier 2: Endurance

Rank 1: Adds 3 points to your endurance score.

Rank 2: Adds an additional 3 points to your endurance score.

Rank 3: Adds an additional 3 points to your endurance score. You cannot be stunned, knocked down, tripped, or disarmed.

Tier 3: Regeneration

This style is equivalent to the auxiliary style Regeneration and can fulfill the same prerequisites.

Rank 1: Your wounds stabilize automatically, because of this your Endurance can never drop below 0 and you no longer require medical care to prevent bleeding to death. You automatically heal a point of damage on all locations each hour.

Rank 2: You automatically heal a point of damage on each location every minute.

Rank 3: You automatically heal a point of damage on each bodily location at the end of every round. You are immune to all diseases, drugs, and poisons.

Agility

Agility

Each rank improves the attribute Agility.

Rank 1 unlocks Super Speed.
Rank 4 unlocks Mobility.
Rank 7 unlocks Active Defense.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the styles Flash Strike or Evasion, or to improve the skillsToughness, Athletics, Stealth, Drive, Defense, or Initiative.

Tier 1: Super Speed

Rank 1: Adds 3 points to your Speed score.

Rank 2: Adds 3 additional points to your Speed score.

Rank 3: Adds 3 additional points to your Speed score.

Tier 2: Mobility

Rank 1: Climbing, jumping, swimming, and traversing rough terrain all have the same speed cost as moving normally. You do not get your ability to use defensive actions denied by climbing or standing in rough terrain. You can move through the space occupied by any enemy without penalty. You cannot be targeted by the free attacks generated by the Finesse style Zone of Denial.

Rank 2: Double the number of successes you roll on climbing, jumping, swimming, and traversing actions. When using a dodge action, you may move 2 spaces for free instead of just 1. Additionally, anytime you are the target of a melee attack you can spend a point of Entanglement to automatically succeed on a dodge attempt, but you must move two spaces when doing so. You can always use this automatic dodge action against melee attacks, but you can also use it against ranged attacks if there is cover within two spaces of you and you move there as part of the action.

Rank 3: Triple the number of successes you roll on climbing, jumping, swimming, and traversing actions. When using the sprint action, the number of squares you can cross is tripled instead of doubled. By using a combination of tumbling, wall-running, and jumping, you can move to any unblocked space as long as there is, at least, 1 space worth of wall or floor somewhere on a path from the start to the destination. This has the same speed cost as moving that distance normally.

Tier 3: Active defense

Rank 1: Add 1 success to all of your dodge rolls.

You can use a Dodge action in between Aim actions without interrupting the aim and losing your accumulated successes.

Rank 2: Add an additional 1 success to all of your dodge rolls.

Any attack that hits you due to a failed dodge roll has all of its location adjustments and critical hit levels removed.

Rank 3: Add an additional 1 success to all of your dodge rolls.

The speed cost for your dodge actions decreases by 1.

Wits

Wits

Each rank improves the attribute Wits.

Rank 1 unlocks Priority.
Rank 4 unlocks Passive Defense.
Rank 7 unlocks Precognition.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the styles Ally Communication, or Leadership, or to improve the skills Stealth, Investigate, Drive, Survival, Charm, Manipulate, or Defense.

Tier 1: Priority

You can spend a point of Entanglement before rolling initiative to speed up your ability to plan and execute actions. Doing so grants the abilities described in the ranks of this style until the end of combat.

Rank 1: While active, your initiative rolls are 1 die higher than normal. When making an initiative roll, roll it twice. If you get a different number on each roll you will have two separate initiative spots. When you have two initiatives you may take an action when each number comes up during a single turn (if you have enough speed for both actions). If you get the same number on both rolls you only have a single normal initiative score.

Rank 2: While active, your initiative rolls are 2 dice higher than normal. When making an initiative roll, instead of rolling it twice as described in rank 1 you can choose a second initiative spot. You can make this choice after everyone has made their roll and you've seen the initiative order. You may take an action on the number you rolled and another action the number you chose, if you have enough speed for both actions.

Rank 3: While active, your initiative rolls are 3 dice higher than normal. When making an initiative roll, make the extra roll described by rank 1 and choose a number as described by rank 2. This will allow you to take up to three separate actions in a single turn, if you have the speed for them.

Tier 2: Passive Defense

Rank 1: Adds 1 point to your passive defense.

Rank 2: Add 1 additional point to your passive defense.

Rank 3: Add 1 additional point to your passive defense.

When you are hit by an attack that you did not attempt to dodge, you may spend a point of Entanglement as a free action in order to remove all location adjustments and critical hit levels from the attack. You may decide if you wish to do this after you know the results of the attack roll and the location roll.

Tier 3: Precognition

You can spend a point of Entanglement to gain precognitive abilities. Doing so grants you the abilities described the ranks in this style for the rest of the scene.

Rank 1: You can always act in a surprise round, regardless of circumstances. When choosing who to target with an attack, you can ask the GM if that target is going to attempt to dodge the attack or not.

Rank 2: You cannot be targeted by Stealth Attack actions. Once per round you can ask the GM what a single visible enemy's next action will be. This information is accurate unless the combat situation changes radically between when you requested the information and the time the enemy gets to act.

Rank 3: You cannot have your ability to dodge denied by surprise or flanking unless you are being physically restrained. When outside of combat, you have a vague sense of what might happen in the next few minutes, because of this you can ask the GM for hints on what is about to happen. How much information you are given is up to the GM, but the information is accurate.

Willpower

Willpower

Each rank improves the attribute Willpower.

Rank 1 unlocks Entanglement +.
Rank 4 unlocks Unlock Arcana.
Rank 7 unlocks a second Unlock Arcana.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the styles Blink, Evocation Extensibility, Filter, Focus Strike, Regeneration, Heal, Combat Healing, Healing Field, Sublimation Storage, or Ward Extensibility, or to improve the skills Toughness, Guts, or Survival.

Tier 1: Entanglement +

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Entanglement: Add 3 points to your Entanglement score.
  • Yau Body Insulation: When inside a cascade event you can choose whether to completely ignore its effects or to experience it normally. In addition, you cannot be the target of the style Telethesia or the artifact parameter inhibit( ) without your consent. The function noo_interface( ) cannot detect you unless you wish it to.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Entanglement: Add 3 additional points to your Entanglement score.
  • Entangled Insulation: When damaged by an evocation attack or a photonic attack, you may reduce the amount of damage done by one for each point of Entanglement you spend.

    When damaged by by an entropic attack, you may reduce its duration by one turn for each point of Entanglement you spend.

    When you trigger the initial effect of a mitigation event you can spend a point of Entanglement to ignore the secondary effects and any further effects from this event.

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Improved Entanglement: Add 3 additional points to your Entanglement score.
  • Diffuse Insulation: You may apply the benefits of Yau Body Insulation and Entangled Insulation to anyone currently adjacent to you. Any Entanglement spent protecting others this way comes from your Entanglement pool.

Tier 2: Sustain Extropy

In addition to the benefits described for each rank, this style also unlocks an arcane style. When you acquire the first rank of this style choose one of these three arcane styles: Abjuration, Evocation, or Sublimation. Each rank of this style unlocks one tier of the selected style.

Communication between the Yau and normal space allows you to create order where once there was nothing. This style harnesses that anti-entropic property to create raw thermodynamic energy. In addition to allowing access to the most overtly energetic arcane styles, this capability also allows your body to resist decay and sustain itself with no external energy sources.

Rank 1: You no longer require oxygen to function comfortably indefinitely. You are also completely immune to the effects of extended exertion, exhaustion, and Crisis Sickness.

Rank 2: You no longer need food or water to stay healthy indefinitely. You are also completely immune to all diseases and any toxins that don't directly corrode flesh.

Rank 3: You no longer require sleep to function comfortably indefinitely. You can only be killed by letting your upper body (location 5) reach destroyed status. Other injuries and blood loss cannot kill you, but they can still render you unconscious, which you will recover from at the normal rate.

Tier 3: Cohesion Extropy

In addition to the benefits described for each rank, this style also unlocks an arcane style. When you acquire the first rank of this style choose one of these three arcane styles: Abjuration, Evocation, or Sublimation. Each rank of this style unlocks one tier of the selected style.

This style refines your ability to counter entropy to the extent that you can completely cheat death. Creating the level of anti-entropy needed to sustain immortality requires near constant two-way communication and manipulation of Yau at all times, because of this you are constantly aware of Yau spirits and they are constantly aware of you. The consequence of this is that as the years go on it becomes increasingly difficult to tell the difference between the real world, the Yau, and the outright hallucinations that will slowly develop.

Rank 1: Your lifespan is extended by a number of decades equal to your Entanglement score. Your appearance maintains itself at the age you are most comfortable with until rapidly aging over the last year of your life.

Rank 2: Your life expectancy is effectively infinite. If you are not killed by physical damage or blood loss you could live healthily forever.

Rank 3: As long as you have at least a single point of Entanglement remaining your body parts cannot reach destroyed status. If a location is dealt enough damage to destroy it, instead it is treated as disabled and stops taking further damage. Massive injuries can still render you unconscious, but they cannot kill or dismember you. You can still die from blood loss, however, if you also have the final rank of the style Sustain Extropy you are immune to blood loss as well. Having 3 ranks of both Extropy styles means you can only be killed by destroying your upper body while your Entanglement is at 0.

Intelligence

Intelligence

Each rank improves the attribute Intelligence.

Rank 1 unlocks Analyze Cascade.
Rank 4 unlocks Unlock Arcana.
Rank 7 unlocks a second Unlock Arcana.

Auxiliary nodes in this class can be used to acquire the styles Embed Function, Embed Cast, Embed Parameter, or Sublimation Storage, or to improve the skills Hardware, Humanities, Medicine, Science, Software, Survival, or Languages.

Tier 1: Analyze Cascade

This style increases your comprehension of the fundamental principles underpinning cascade interactions between the Yau and normal space. This knowledge allows you to discern details about cascade events, artifacts, and styles than would otherwise be available.

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Analyze Artifact: By spending an hour closely examining an artifact you can identify and enumerate all of its functions and parameters.
  • Detect Entanglement: You can tell if someone has styles and roughly how much Entanglement they have just by looking at them.
  • Detect Event: You can sense the exact boundaries of any cascade event.
  • Locate Artifact: Whenever an artifact is within a number of meters equal to your Perception score multiplied by the number of ranks you have in this style, you will immediately become aware of its presence and the exact direction to it.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Analyze Event: You can tell the type, nature, intensity, and probable origin of any cascade event by being near it or in it.
  • Detect Aperture: You can see any objects that have been stored in the Yau by the style Sublimation Storage, the function aperture( ), or any other means.
  • Style Comparator: You can tell just by looking at someone whether they have more styles or fewer styles than you.

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Detect Anomaly: You can immediately tell if anything you see originates from, or is manipulated from, somewhere outside of natural physical space. This ability can detect if something came from a different worldline, was assembled by Yau based entity, is controlled Surrogate Proxy, Delegate Proxy, or Telethesia, was created by Coalesce Avatar or Propagate Avatar, or has an otherwise unnatural origin.
  • Eavesdrop: Anytime someone uses the style Ally Communication or the style Telethesia within a kilometer you will immediately become aware of the identities of the participants and, if you wish, the content of the information being transmitted. This ability does not allow you to modify or participate in the any communication that you happen to overhear.
  • Enstylization Quotient: You can tell just by looking at someone how many styles they have and what style class they have the most ranks in.
  • Predict Event: You can predict roughly where and when the next cascade event or worldline incursion within a few kilometers will occur, as well as what the nature of the event will be.

Tier 2: Coalesce Avatar

In addition to the benefits described for each rank, this style also unlocks an arcane style. When you acquire the first rank of this style choose one of these three arcane styles: Chromodynamism, Electromagnetism, or Gravitonertia. Each rank of this style unlocks one tier of the selected style.

This style allows you to persist after death as a mobile entity in the Yau that is capable of slowly building a new physical body out raw materials. After the death of your physical body, your consciousness can drift around the Yau with a rough sense of the landscape and chemical composition of nearby physical space. Outside of a vague sense of how secluded an area is, you are mostly unaware of the actions of people in nearby physical space.

Once you have chosen the site where you wish to build your new body the process begins, and you are no longer conscious until the new body is complete. If this site is disturbed, the process is halted and must be started again elsewhere. A person disturbing the site will discover what appears to be your decaying corpse. In addition to privacy, the site also requires raw materials. While you could complete the process just from pulling carbon dioxide and moisture from the air in an empty room, doing so would take an extremely long time. A site rich in water, organic carbon compounds, and other trace minerals, such as in mud or standing water, will allow you to build a body much faster.

This style has consequences that are unnoticeable at first, but after you create and occupy a new body your appearance and mannerisms start appearing more unnatural. Each new body you create is increasingly uncanny, it may be beautiful or ugly, but it is always somewhat unnerving to others. At first this may be hidden by makeup or other styles, but after creating several new bodies, it rarely goes unnoticed.

Rank 1: With a site rich in organic matter you can build a body in 3 months, without one it would take 6 months.

Rank 2: With a site rich in organic matter you can build a body in 3 weeks, without one it would take 3 months. Instead of growing a body that looks like your previous one, you may choose any normal human physical appearance for it.

Rank 3: With a rich site you can build a body in 3 days, without one it would take 3 weeks. When building a new body, you may redistribute the original attribute points you chose at character creation in any way you see fit. Attribute points granted by styles cannot be redistributed.

Tier 3: Propagate Avatar

In addition to the benefits described for each rank, this style also unlocks an arcane style. When you acquire the first rank of this style choose one of these three arcane styles: Chromodynamism, Electromagnetism, or Gravitonertia. Each rank of this style unlocks one tier of the selected style.

This style allows you to persist after death as an entity in the Yau and build a new body with the same benefits, requirements, and consequences described by the style Coalesce Avatar. If you don't also have ranks in Coalesce Avatar building a body takes a full year.

Rank 1: You can create a second body for yourself before your death. This second body takes just as long to build as normal. Once it is finished it can be stored so that you can begin occupying it an hour after your death, regardless of where you died. While this body is growing or being stored it is indistinguishable from a your own corpse, so it should be carefully hidden. Any body you have previously occupied is no longer viable and begins to rot. You must physically visit the site to begin the building the body and you can only have a single extra body growing or being stored.

If you allow it, any body you create may instead be used by any sentient being in the Yau. This includes anyone with any ranks in any of the Avatar, Extropy, or Proxy styles that has died and also anyone visiting the Yau via the style Sublimation. No one can use one of your bodies without your knowledge and permission. Anyone other than you that is using a body that you created loses a point of Entanglement every hour. If someone using one of your bodies runs out of Entanglement, is fatally wounded, or decides to leave the body, then the body is no longer viable and begins to rot.

Rank 2: You can create any number of bodies for yourself before your death. Each one can be stored in a different location, but you must physically visit the each location to being creating a body there. These bodies can be stored indefinitely once complete. You can transfer to any of these bodies at any time instead of waiting for your death, doing so takes an hour. After a body has been occupied once it is no longer viable and begins to rot.

Rank 3: There can now be multiple instances of your consciousness active in different bodies that you have prepared. Each concurrently active instance of you has an equal share of your Intelligence score rounded down. Tier 1: Induce Cascade

This style allows you to create several subtle cascade effects that can be very useful in manipulating those around you or in creating full blown localized cascade events.

Any of these abilities that require an expenditure of Entanglement to activate, also require a minute of concentration and cannot be activated during combat. Some of these effects can have their size and duration increased by spending additional Entanglement. These extensible abilities start with a duration of a minute and a size of a single space that you can see, but each additional point of Entanglement increases the duration by an hour or the size from one space, to a room (or an area up to 9 meters across), to a house (or an area up to 30 meters across). Sizes larger than that cannot be achieved by a single person.

Rank 1 grants the following capabilities:

  • Cavitation Event: You can spend a point of Entanglement to collapse a small pocket of air into the Yau, producing either a loud phantom sound or flash of light at any point you can see. This cannot be used to create voices, images, or otherwise convey complex information.
  • Induce Dissociation: You can spend a point of Entanglement to cause an area (extensible in size or duration) to temporarily have mentally dissociative properties. Anyone with available points of Entanglement will notice the effect of being in the area, but be otherwise immune to it's effects. Anyone else in the area will be stricken with a sudden intense anxiety and be required to make Guts roll. Failing to get 3 successes on this roll results in intense fear and paranoia while failing to get any successes on this roll results in a very physical panic attack. Leaving the area or the duration ending will result in these mental effects quickly abating.

Rank 2 grants the following capabilities:

  • Disentangle: You can spend a point of Entanglement to prevent everyone in an area from forming long-term memories by partially cutting communications between their brains and Yau Bodies. The area affected can be extended normally, but the effect occurs instantly and spending extra Entanglement on duration instead increases the the amount of lost time where no memories can be formed. The lost time is centered on the time this effect was triggered, so if you spend 4 points of entanglement to get a duration of 4 hours everyone effected will have no memory of 2 hours prior and 2 hours after the effect. People act normally during the duration of the lost time, but afterwards will have no memory of events that occurred during it. Anyone with available points of Entanglement is immune to this effect and instead loses a single point of Entanglement.
  • Élan Vital: Your cascade bandwidth is driven by your persona instead of raw will alone. Due to this, if your Charisma score is higher than your Willpower score replace the Willpower component of your Entanglement score with Charisma.
  • Induce Minor Event: You can spend a point of Entanglement to induce either a positive polarity event or a negative polarity event in an area (extensible in size or duration).

Rank 3 grants the following capabilities:

  • Induce Deep Event: You can spend a point of entanglement to induce either a nonlocality event or a total wavefunction collapse event in an area. Due to the intensity of these cascade events, the Entanglement costs of extending their area or duration are doubled.
  • Sever Entanglement: Similar to the Disentangle ability of the previous rank, this ability uses a point of entanglement to cut the connection between the brain and the Yau Body, however this ability severs the connection so thoroughly that everyone in the affected area is immediately rendered unconscious for the entire duration of the effect. Both area and duration are extensible at the normal rate. The effect comes on so quickly that people fall down on the spot, potentially causing injury if they are standing or in motion. Anyone with available points of Entanglement is immune to this effect and instead loses a single point of Entanglement. Those affected cannot be roused by any medical means, however someone with Entanglement can spend a point of it as a full action with a speed cost of 3 to awaken an adjacent person.
  • Ally Communication: You gain a bonus rank of the auxiliary style Ally Communication, this rank stacks with any ranks you may already have in this style.

Tier 2: Surrogate Proxy

In addition to the benefits described for each rank, this style also unlocks an arcane style. When you acquire the first rank of this style choose one of these three Arcane Styles: Schizosomata, Pneumaplegia, or Telethesia. Each rank of this style unlocks one tier of the selected Arcane Style.

Both this style and the style Delegate Proxy allow you to persist after death as mobile entity in the Yau that is aware of the movements of people in nearby physical space and is capable of possessing the bodys of other people. The chosen host must not have any styles or Entanglement and must be sleeping nearby when you attempt to possess them. Possession takes an hour to complete and once done you experience the host's senses fully any time they are awake. You cannot control the host body without this ranks in this style, and you cannot leave the host body without ranks in Delegate Proxy. You may gain style points with which to improve Proxy styles if the person you are bonded with does anything worth earning them.

Any time that you are not in control of the host body you can only communicate with your host or others by using the auxiliary style Ally Communication. If you do attempt to communicate with your host they have no reason to believe you are anything other than an auditory hallucination unless you can convince them otherwise.

The consequence of this style is fear. No matter what body you are in people fear you like they would a predator. You are still as charismatic as before and still capable of making friends, but a stranger's initial reaction to you is always fear and intimidation. This effect is very mild at first but it increases each time you possess a new body and as you get used to the ethical ramifications of doing so.

Rank 1: While your host is asleep you can spend a point of Entanglement to take control of their body. While you are in control of your host you do not get the benefits of any of your other styles and your attributes are a mix of yours and the host's. You have the host's Agility, Finesse, Perception, Strength, Stamina, and Wits scores but your starting Charisma, Intelligence, and Willpower scores. When your host begins to wake up, you will lose consciousness for the next 8 hours regardless of where you are. As long this happens in the same place they fell asleep and you leave no evidence of your activities the host will not be aware that anything happened. Additionally, while your host is awake you can spend Entanglement to influence any rolls your host attempts (you can turn dice into automatic successes or automatic successes into dice) or to assist in healing them.

Rank 2: You gain the benefit of your styles while you are in control of your host. If you chose a host that is, or becomes, comatose or brain dead you are now completely in control of the body.

Rank 3: You can evict the personality and consciousness from a host body you have bonded with, this renders the body completely yours.

Tier 3: Delegate Proxy

In addition to the benefits described for each rank, this style also unlocks an Arcane Style. When you acquire the first rank of this style choose one of these three Arcane Styles: Schizosomata, Pneumaplegia, or Telethesia. Each rank of this style unlocks one tier of the selected Arcane Style.

This style grants the same starting benefits and consequences as Surrogate Proxy, but without ranks in Surrogate Proxy you cannot take control of your host. The only way to gain control of the host is to buy ranks in Surrogate Proxy, even if this requires you waiting for the host to do something worthy of you gaining the needed style points. You only experience the senses of the host you are currently occupying, even if you have a connection to multiple hosts.

Rank 1: At any time, you can leave your host body and attempt to bond with a different host (the bonding process always takes an hour). If you had previously evicted the consciousness of your host it will slowly return to them over the course of the next 24 hours, but they will have no memory of any events that occurred while you were in control. You can communicate telepathically with your host, even if you do not have the style Ally Communication. The host is inclined to believe almost anything you tell it that isn't blatantly and obviously false.

Rank 2: The last host body you occupied retains a connection to you, you can switch between it and the host you currently occupy at any time. Switching takes a minute if both hosts are adjacent or an hour if the hosts are at any distance from each other. You can communicate with both your current and previous hosts at any distance. Additionally, while you cannot directly control your host without Surrogate Proxy, both your current and previous host are now loyal to you and will follow your directions to the best of their ability. In order to resist your orders they must tie your roll of Charisma + Manipulation with their roll of Willpower + Guts, however, they may get bonus successes on this roll if the order is strongly against their best interest or wishes.

Rank 3: You retain a connection to all of your most recent hosts, up to a number of hosts equal to your total Charisma score. This group of hosts all act as their original personality, but they are loyal to you as per the description of rank 2 of this style.

Auxiliary Styles

Auxiliary nodes are unlocked by buying the first rank of each tier of a core style. Each auxiliary node can either be used to improve one of the skills related to the style or to acquire an auxiliary style.

Each auxiliary style has availability requirements that dictate what style classes and tiers can acquire them. For example, if an auxiliary style's availability is listed as "Wits tier 1 or Charisma tier 2," then it can be slotted into any auxiliary node of the Wits style class and the 2nd or 3rd tier node of the Charisma style class.

Many auxiliary styles also have a prerequisite style that you must have ranks in before you can acquire that style. If the prerequisite lists a style but not a rank, you just need at least one rank in it, but if it lists a specific rank you must have at least that many ranks in the prerequisite.

Unless the description says otherwise, auxiliary styles cannot be acquired more than once.

Rank 3: When creating your barrier you can spend a single extra point of Entanglement to choose what it filters on a case by case basis. Your barriers do not prevent the passage of things in the Yau unless you spend an extra point of Entanglement when creating them.

The appearance of barriers varies with the individual creating them, and you do have a degree of control over the cosmetic appearance of each one you create. However, unless you have ranks in the style Filter, your barriers are always transparent enough not to hinder vision but easily visible due to a faint color and luminosity.

Tier 1: Create Ward

Rank 1: Your barriers can absorb 1 points of damage before dissipating.

Rank 2: Your barriers can absorb 2 points of damage before dissipating.

Rank 3: Your barriers can absorb 3 points of damage before dissipating.

Tier 2: Resistant Ward

Rank 4: Your barriers can absorb 4 points of damage before dissipating.

Rank 5: Your barriers can absorb 5 points of damage before dissipating.

Rank 6: Your barriers can absorb 6 points of damage before dissipating.

Tier 3: Bulletproof Ward

Rank 7: Your barriers can absorb 9 points of damage before dissipating.

Rank 8: Your barriers can absorb a number of points of damage equal to your Guts + 9.

Rank 9: Your barriers can absorb a number of points of damage equal to your Willpower + Guts + 9.

Evocation

Unlocked by Willpower.

This style can be enhanced by the auxiliary style Evocation Extensibility.

This style allows you to execute an evocation attack that blasts the target with a torrent of energy. Evocation attacks are combat actions that cost 1 point of Entanglement and have a speed cost and range determined by this style's rank. Evocation attacks always hit their target in the bodily location of your choice, but cannot critical. You cannot be hurt by you own evocation attacks but an ally can be.

The cosmetic appearance of evocation attacks is different for every character. When first acquiring this style decide what your character's evocation attacks look like. No matter what form yours take, the higher the rank you use of this style, the more noticeable the attack is. It may be possible discretely use a rank 1 or 2 evocation attack, but the rank 8 or 9 version is always loud, overtly visible, and impossible to hide.

When using an evocation attack, you can decide what rank you want to use. In order to make the using this style less noticeable, you can opt to use it with the damage output of any of the ranks lower than the highest rank you possess. Doing so still utilizes the improved speed and range that any higher rank you possess grants.

Tier 1: Evocation Attack

Rank 1: Your evocation attacks deal 1+1 damage, have a speed cost of 8, and a range of either 3 or your Charisma score (whichever is greater).

Rank 2: Your evocation attacks deal 2+1 damage.

Rank 3: Your evocation attacks deal 3+1 damage.

Tier 2: Improved Evocation

Rank 4: The range of your evocation attacks is increased by 3, and they now have a speed cost of 6.

Rank 5: Your evocation attacks deal 3+2 damage.

Rank 6: Your evocation attacks deal 3+3 damage.

Tier 3: Maximized Evocation

Rank 7: The range of your evocation attacks is again increased by 3, and they now have a speed cost of 4.

Rank 8: Your evocation attacks deal either 3+3 damage or Willpower + 3 damage, whichever is greater.

Rank 9: Your evocation attacks deal either 3+3 damage or Willpower + Guts damage, whichever is greater.

Sublimation

Unlocked by Willpower.

This style can be enhanced by the auxiliary styles Sublimation Storage and Blink.

This style allows you to enter the Yau; a parallel realm that mirrors normal space. Entering the Yau costs 1 point of Entanglement, and entering or exiting typically takes a minute of concentration, preventing access during combat except where specifically listed otherwise. While inside the Yau you can pass through objects that are mobile (such as crowds or traffic) but not stationary objects (such as walls or infrequently used doors).

In the Yau your senses do no operate normally, rendering you blind and deaf. Instead you have an omnidirectional sense called chromaception that can detect the motion and position of anything that is completely within the Yau that you would have line of sight to. You can only roughly detect objects and motion in physical space within a meter of you, however if you have ranks in the style Chromaception this range increases to match the range of the situational awareness granted by that style.

Tier 1: Access Shallows

Rank 1: You can transport any single object that you can hold into the Yau for undetectable storage and then retrieve it at a later time. You can only have a single item stored at a time and you must be the only person touching an item to store it. Storing or retrieving an item costs 1 point of Entanglement and cannot be done in combat.

Rank 2: You can send you senses into the Yau to observe anything that happens in it near your current location.

Rank 3: Your can send your consciousness into the Yau and move around undetected by anyone in normal space. Entering the Yau requires you to leave your unconscious body behind in normal space. You must return to your physical body in order to exit the Yau.

Tier 2: Sublimate Body

Rank 4: When you enter the Yau you can bring your physical body with you instead of leaving it behind. You still must still exit the Yau in the same location that you entered it.

Rank 5: You can now exit the Yau anywhere you can access while inside of it.

Rank 6: You can go deeper into the Yau, which allows you to circumnavigate previously impassable obstacles. Any object that isn't completely stationary all of the time, including most locked doors, can be passed through.

Tier 3: Access Deep Yau

Rank 7: While in the Yau you can pass through anything that has moved at least once within the 10 years.

You can now bring others into the Yau with you. Each extra point of Entanglement spent allows an adjacent willing person to enter the Yau with you. These people must stay within sight of you and exit the Yau when and where you do, otherwise, they will lose consciousness until they eventually return to normal space in a location determined by the GM.

Rank 8: While in the Yau you can pass through anything that has moved at least once within the last 30 years.

You can now exit the Yau in combat. Doing so is a combat action that uses 3 speed. You can combine exiting the Yau with an attack. The speed cost of doing so is the cost of the attack + 3. This attack costs 1 point of Entanglement and is always treated as a stealth attack.

Rank 9: While in the Yau you can pass through anything that has moved at least once within the 90 years.

You can use a defensive action costing 3 speed and 1 Entanglement to phase partially into the Yau and avoid one attack without needing to roll. When you use this defensive action, if you have ranks in the auxiliary style Blink you can increase the speed cost of the action by 3 to end the action either in the Yau or in any space you could travel to using your ranks in Blink. You can’t use action this if you are denied the ability to use defensive actions.

Pneumaplegia

Unlocked by Charisma.

This style allows you to create special localized cascade events, called mitigation events, that can slow, stun, and hinder anyone that enters them. When creating a mitigation event choose what spaces are affected by the event, you can choose any space you can clearly see and this choice remains set for the entire duration of the event. At the first rank, only a single space can be affected by the event and it lasts for 3 rounds. Creating a mitigation event is a combat action with a speed cost equal to the number of spaces the event affects. Any space affected contains a visible haze that anyone can notice by getting two successes on a Perception + Awareness roll. You can dismiss your mitigation events at any time, but you can not create more than one mitigation event at the same time.

Anyone (friend or foe) entering a space affected by a mitigation event triggers an immediate effect, and then a secondary effect that occurs if and when the space is exited. Remaining in the space for any duration has no additional effects. In addition to any secondary effects described by the ranks of this style, the secondary effect drains a number of points of speed from the exiting person equal to your rank in this style. Anyone making a basic move action that passes through an affected space can choose whether they wish to stop in the space when the immediate effect triggers, or continue on and trigger the secondary effect. Anyone using a special move action (such as Balance, Climb, Jump, Sprint, Swim, or Charge) that passes through an affected space is only stopped if the immediate effect reduces their speed to 0, otherwise, they must continue and immediately trigger the secondary effect. Exiting an affected space into another affected space still triggers the secondary effect. Anyone exiting an affected space is immune to all mitigation events until the start of the next round, but the affected space can still trigger on anyone else during its duration. Any damage a mitigation event may cause ignores armor, shield( ) functions, and any other source of damage protection.

Mitigation events you create have a potency level that is determined by your rank in this style. At the first rank, potency is 0 but it increases in further ranks. Anyone triggering the secondary effect of the event must lose a number of points of Endurance or Entanglement equal to the event's level of potency. The triggering person chooses the number of points of each resource to lose, as long as the sum is equal to the event potency.

Tier 1: Mitigating Event

Rank 1: The immediate effect of your mitigation events drains a point of Speed. Anything in the Yau that triggers the immediate effect is instantly dumped back into physical space. The secondary effect reduces the current durability of a shield( ) function to zero.

Rank 2: Your mitigation events last for 6 rounds and have a potency of 1.

When creating a mitigation event, you can decide if it has positive effects instead of the normal negative effects. This choice cannot be changed for the duration of the event. If the event is positive it has an immediate effect that occurs when anyone enters an affected space but no secondary effect triggered by exiting. A positive event can trigger on any number of people that enter it, but any one person can only trigger a positive event once for it's entire duration. When triggered, a positive event does none of the other negative effects described in this style, but instead the person triggering it regains number of points of Speed equal to your rank in this style and then may choose a number of points up to the potency level of this event to distribute between regaining Endurance or gaining Entanglement.

Rank 3: Your mitigation events can affect up to 2 adjacent spaces and have a potency level of 2. Additionally, you can decide if your event is easily visible to all, completely invisible, or visible only to those that pass the normal Perception + Alertness roll.

Tier 2: Attenuating Event

Rank 4: Your mitigation events last for 9 rounds. The immediate effect now drains 2 points of speed. For each level of potency, the person triggering the secondary event may now opt to take a point of damage on every bodily location instead of losing a point of Endurance or Entanglement.

If the event is positive, the person triggering it can opt to heal a point on each bodily location instead of gaining a point of Endurance or Entanglement.

Rank 5: Your mitigation events can affect up to 3 contiguous (each space must share a side or corner with at least one other space) spaces and have a potency level of 3. Anyone triggering the secondary effect must immediately end their turn and take no further actions until their next turn.

Rank 6: Your mitigation events last for a minute and have a potency level of 4.

Additionally, if the event is positive anyone inside one of its affected spaces can move to any other space that is in or adjacent to an affected space as a move action costing 1 speed.

Tier 3: Halting Event

Rank 7: Your mitigation events can affect up to 4 spaces; each space must be within 3 spaces of the nearest affected space. The immediate effect now drains 3 points of speed. Anyone triggering the secondary effect is stunned, immediately ending their turn and preventing any further actions for the rest of the round.

Rank 8: Your mitigation events last for an hour and have a potency level of 5. When creating a negative event, you can decide if either has normal immediate and secondary effects, or if the secondary effects are triggered instead of the immediate effect upon entering the space. Events that trigger this way do not have their normal immediate effects and nothing is triggered when exiting them.

Rank 9: Your mitigation events can affect up to 6 spaces; each space must be within 9 spaces of the nearest affected space. Your mitigation events are now extensible; each extra point of Entanglement spent when creating one increases its duration by an hour, the number of affected spaces by one, or the potency level by one.

Schizosomata

Unlocked by Charisma.

This style allows you to change the way you are perceived. It can make you less noticeable, change your physical appearance, grant you the temporary skills needed to succeed in someone else's role, or even make you outright invisible.

Tier 1: Fade

Rank 1: You can spend a point of Entanglement to activate this style for a scene. While it is active you become less noticeable. In any environment where seeing a person isn't alarming, all people that see you assume you are supposed to be there or that you are someone else’s problem. You do not become invisible, people simply just feel no need to pay attention to your presence. In more restricted environments you may need to make a Charisma + Stealth roll for this effect to work, the difficulty of which is determined by how secure the area is and if people are actively searching for you. Passing unnoticed through a public space will be trivially easy, but passing unnoticed through a place where the presence of any person would be a cause for alarm will be difficult. If you act highly unusual for the environment, take any aggressive action, or combat begins, then this effect ends. Conversely, you can choose to instead spend a point of Entanglement to have the opposite effect, causing you to become extremely noticeable, stand out in a crowd, and become impossible to ignore.

Rank 2: You can modify your appearance over time to become an idealized version of yourself that is as physically attractive as you wish to be. At the GM's discretion, this may reduce the difficulty on many social rolls.

Rank 3: You can cause people to be unable to remember your actions. Each point of Entanglement spent causes this effect for an hour. Everyone that witnesses you during this effect has selective memory loss of your presence. Those affected do not experience lost time, they just cannot recall your presence or actions during this time. If an action you take has overt lasting consequences, it will be attributed to the next most plausible source instead of you. Video cameras will still capture your actions during this time, however, your face will appear too blurry to identify. Anyone with Entanglement loses 3 points of it if they observe you during this effect. If their remaining Entanglement is higher than the number of ranks you have in this style they will still remember your actions.

Tier 2: Shift

Rank 4: You can spend a point of Entanglement to become completely invisible. Doing so in combat is a full action costing 8 speed and results in you becoming invisible at the start of the next round. If you move faster than 1 space per round, make loud noises, if anyone enters your space, or you take any aggressive action you immediately become visible again. Attacking anyone while invisible automatically counts as a stealth attack action if you wish it to. Anything in the Yau can still detect your Yau body while you are invisible.

Rank 5: You can make minor changes to your appearance over the course of 8 hours by spending a point of Entanglement. Minor changes include facial structure, skin and hair color, up to an inch of height, and up to 10 pounds of weight. When using this ability to disguise yourself as a specific person, you must succeed at Charisma + Charm rolls to be a convincing double. The more drastic the changes in your appearance and the better the person you are trying to fool knows the person you are trying to imitate, the greater the difficulty of this roll.

Rank 6: If you can currently see someone that has skill or attribute with a higher score than yours, you can spend up to 3 points of Entanglement to increase your score in that trait by up to 3 points for the rest of the scene. Activating this ability takes about a minute. You cannot have more than a single trait increased this way at any given time.

Tier 3: Emulate

Rank 7: Becoming invisible is now a combat action costing 4 speed and occurs immediately. You can also move at any speed, share a space with a person, and make any noise while remaining invisible. Only attacking someone reveals you, but doing so grants you an automatic stealth attack if you wish it to. Anything in the Yau can no longer detect your Yau body while you are invisible.

Rank 8: You can completely change your appearance over the course of an hour by spending a point of Entanglement. You can make a perfect duplicate of a person of any race, age, build, or gender. Every 3 inches difference in height or 30 pounds difference in weight requires you to spend an additional Entanglement point to complete the transformation. If you are attempting to pass as another person, your transformation is flawless enough to fool even the person’s closest friends. People will only become suspicious if you act grossly out of character.

Rank 9: If you can currently see someone that possesses a specialty style, arcane style, or auxiliary style that you do not, you can spend up to 3 points of Entanglement to increase your rank in that style by up to 3 points for the rest of the scene. Activating this ability takes about a minute. You cannot have more than a single style increased this way at any given time. You need to meet the prerequisites of any auxiliary style you increase this way, but you do not need to meet the requirements for specialty and arcane styles.

Telethesia

Unlocked by Charisma.

This style allows you to sense distant locations, read minds, and at high levels even see into the past and control the thoughts of others. Any use of this style costs a point of Entanglement and takes a few minutes of concentration (and thus cannot be used in combat). When using this style to read or influence another mind, it can be resisted by rolling the target’s Willpower + Guts against your Charisma + Guts. If the target has any Entanglement add their current number of available Entanglement points to the number of dice in their resistance roll. The more guarded the information you seek or the more your suggestions conflict with the target's nature and desires, the more successes you will need to beat the target by to overcome their resistance. Any specific use of this style lasts for the duration of the scene. Changing the targeted person or location of this style requires spending another point of Entanglement.

Tier 1: Empathic Connection

Rank 1: You can use this style to see what is happening at any location that is out in the open and within 3 kilometers of you. You only gain vision of the area, no other senses. This rank also grants you complete immunity to the effects of this style or any other effect that could allow someone to read or influence your mind.

Rank 2: You can attempt to become aware of a target’s overall state of mind, general emotional state, and stress level. Additionally, you gain a bonus rank of the auxiliary style Ally Communication that stacks with any ranks you may already have in it.

Rank 3: You can attempt to stimulate a target’s brain to briefly release a single specific neurotransmitter. This will allow you to create in the target a basic emotion or vague sensation. The greater the difference between the target’s current emotional state and the desired sensation the greater the number of additional successes the telethesia roll requires.

Tier 2: Telepathic Connection

Rank 4: You can use this style to sense what is happening at any location that is within 30 kilometers of you. This location must be somewhat accessible in the shallow Yau, any permanent barrier still blocks your senses but anything that has moved within a day does not. A locked door is only an obstacle if it hasn't been opened that recently. This rank grants sight, sound, and smell at the chosen location.

Rank 5: You can attempt to gain access to the entirety of the target’s internal monologue. If you succeed you will know any thought the target has while you are using this style on them. Additionally, you gain a bonus rank of the auxiliary style Ally Communication that stacks with any ranks you may already have in it.

Rank 6: You can attempt to implant a specific thought or suggestion in the target’s mind. The suggestion must be simple, no more than a single statement or sentiment. The more successes you beat the target by on the telethesia roll the harder it is for them to resist the suggestion. A suggestion that differs from the target’s nature and desires, is very strange, or is very specific requires more additional successes to stick.

Tier 3: Asynchronous Connection

Rank 7: You can use this style to sense what is happening at any location that is within 300 kilometers of you. This location must be accessible in via a moderately deep trip through the Yau, any permanent barrier still blocks your senses but anything that has moved within the last month does not. Additionally, you can observe the events at a location either in the present time, or at any chosen time in the last month.

Rank 8: You can attempt to read a target's memories. The older, more private, more embarrassing, or otherwise guarded a memory is, the more successes you'll need to beat the target by to access it. Memories from this day requires 1 success, from this week takes 2, this month takes 3, this year takes 4, this decade 5, and an additional point for each previous decade. Additionally, you gain a bonus rank of the auxiliary style Ally Communication that stacks with any ranks you may already have in it.

Rank 9: You can attempt to implant a series of instructions or artificial memories in the target’s mind. The more successes you beat the target by on the telethesia roll the harder it is for them to resist the instructions or the more vivid, detailed, and realistic the memories can be. With many extra successes these memories can be complex, inserting new people and phases of life are both feasible. An instruction or memory that differs from the target’s nature and desires, is very strange, or is very complex requires more additional successes to stick.

Chromodynamism

Unlocked by Intelligence.

This style deals with the ability to manipulate the properties and compositions of matter. When using this style to transform matter, you can effect up to a cubic meter of any unattended non-living substance that you touch. Spending an additional point of Entanglement effects an additional cubic meter of substance.

This style also allows you to make entropic attacks that destabilizes the atomic structures in a target. The target will experience internal hemorrhaging, abraded flesh, and decaying bones. At rank 1, these attacks are a full action costing 8 speed and 1 Entanglement and require you to touch the target. These attacks always hit and ignore the protection granted by armor, shield( ) functions, and any other style or function that reduces the amount of damage an attack does. These attacks deal no immediate damage, but after the affected person's next turn they take a point of damage on every bodily location. If they take no actions on their turn they do not take damage at that time, but if they take no actions for a whole round they are still dealt damage at the end of the round. Those affected are dealt damage a number of times equal to the number of ranks you have in this style. For example, if you have 7 ranks in this style the target will be dealt a point of damage after each of their next 7 turns. You cannot target a space containing someone who is already being damaged by an entropic attack with another entropic attack. If combat ends any remaining damage due to a target happens over the course of the next minute. You can opt to end the damage being caused by one of your entropic attacks at any time.

With the exception of entropic attacks and the passive ability you gain at rank 2, all applications of this style take a few minutes of concentration, and thus cannot be done in combat.

Tier 1: Stability Manipulation

Rank 1: You can spend a point of Entanglement to gain the ability to sense detailed information about the chemical composition and molecular and atomic structure of any object you can examine. This ability lasts for the rest of the scene. This ability allows you to examine things on a sub-atomic level which, among other applications, allows you to sense what is happening around you in the Yau.

Rank 2: You naturally solidify the sub-atomic structures in the area around yourself at all times. This prevents anything from entering or exiting the Yau within 9 spaces of you and prevents anything from moving through the Yau within 3 spaces of you unless you are aware of it and choose to allow it. This ability is passively occurring at all times, you do not need to use any time, actions, or Entanglement to activate it.

Rank 3: You can spend Entanglement to regain a point of Endurance or sacrifice a point of Endurance to gain a point of Entanglement. Your entropic attacks no longer require you to touch the target, instead, you can now target anyone within a range of 2.

Tier 2: Transmute Substance

Rank 4: You can spend a point of Entanglement to temporarily modify the chemical properties (reactivity, acidity, etc) or physical state (solid, liquid, gas) of a homogenous substance.

Rank 5: You can lose a point of Entanglement or Endurance to give a point of Entanglement or Endurance to anyone adjacent to you, or alternatively you can steal a point of an adjacent person's Entanglement or Endurance to gain a point of either yourself. The resource gained does not have to be the same resource as the one spent or stolen. Additionally, your entropic attacks are now a combat action instead of a full action.

Rank 6: You can spend a point of Entanglement to permanently transmute any homogeneous substance into any other homogeneous substance. This allows such feats as lead into gold or water into wine.

Tier 3: Transmute Structure

Rank 7: You can spend a point of Entanglement to coalesce a solid object made of a homogeneous substance out of ambient gasses. Your entropic attacks can now target any space within a range of 3.

Rank 8: You can spend a point of Entanglement to change materials into complex composite or structured materials. This would allow you to turn a substance into anything from specific metal alloys, to woven carbon strands, to layered silicon substrates.

Rank 9: You can spend a point of Entanglement to turn any substance into any other. You can also control the shape and structure of an object you are transmuting with enough skill to transform any substance into a functional machine. The only restriction is that you can only create objects that you have the knowledge of how to create by mundane means. For example, if you had sufficient skill as a mechanic you could turn water into an engine or if you knew enough about guns you could mold a brick into a working loaded revolver.

Electromagnetism

Unlocked by Intelligence.

This style allows you to manipulate the electromagnetic spectrum including light, radio waves, x-rays, electricity, and magnetism. Until rank 9, you can only effect a small amount of electromagnetism equal to roughly a human sized sunbeam, enough electricity to do about a point of damage to all locations on a target, or a 6-inch rare earth magnet. Until rank 8, the duration of any effects of this style is a number of minutes equal to your Willpower, but each additional point of Entanglement spent will double this time.

Additionally, you can use this style to do an photonic attack as a combat action with a speed cost of 6. Doing so costs a point of Entanglement and blasts a foe with focused light or electricity. This attack can target anyone within a range equal to the number of ranks you have in this style. This attack always hits the target in the bodily location you choose, but can never critical. At the first rank this attack does 1 point of damage to a single bodily location on a target. If a target is protected by a shield( ) function they are completely immune to photonic attacks as long as the shield has at least 1 point of durability.

Any application of this style requires a point of Entanglement to activate. With the exception of photonic attacks, using this style takes a minimum of a minute of concentration, and cannot be used in combat because of this.

Tier 1: Redirect Charge

Rank 1: You can activate this style to gain the ability to sense the entire electromagnetic spectrum for the rest of the scene. This allows you such utilities as night vision, heat vision, or x-ray vision, the ability to see radio waves, and sense the presence of any electromagnetic field. If you concentrate you can gather accurate details about the electrical and magnetic properties of anything you can see.

Rank 2: You can alter the direction of a small amount of electromagnetism. This can be used for anything from short-circuiting an electrical device, shrouding a single space in darkness by causing light to travel around it, or reversing the polarity of a magnetic source.

Rank 3: Your photonic attacks deal 2 points of damage to the bodily location you choose as well as both bodily locations adjacent to this location. For example, choosing a target's lower body(location 4) would deal 2 damage to this spot, 2 damage to the upper body(location 5) and 2 damage to the main arm(location 3).

Tier 2: Transmute Waveform

Rank 4: You can transform a small amount of one type of electromagnetism into another type. For example, this could be used to convert static into enough light to see by or to turn sunlight into a strong magnetic field.

Rank 5: Your photonic attacks deal 3 points of damage to all bodily locations on the target.

Rank 6: You can create or destroy a small amount of any type of electromagnetism. For example, this could render a battery or magnet useless or turn any object into one, induce or prevent any current in a wire, create a blinding light source from nothing, or render anything in a single space completely invisible.

Tier 3: Massive Propagation

Rank 7: Your photonic attacks now target a space instead of an enemy and deal points 4 damage to all locations of everyone within a radius of 1.

Rank 8: Any non-combat effect created by this style now lasts for the rest of the scene. Each additional point of Entanglement spent extends the effect for 24 hours.

Rank 9: The size of the affects you can create is no longer limited. Affecting a larger area simply requires you to spend more time and Entanglement to create the effect. Several square meters would take about 3 points and 5 minutes while a city block could take around 6 points and a half hour. While there is no upward limit, the GM will factor in the size, complexity, and intensity of the effect you want to create and assign the appropriate costs for it. Additionally, your photonic attacks now deal 4 points of damage to all bodily locations on everyone in a radius of 2.

Gravitonertia

Unlocked by Intelligence.

This style grants you the ability to manipulate mass and momentum. Ranks of this style allow you to levitate and move any stationary and unattended non-living object. Starting or continuing to levitate an object is a full action with a speed cost of 3 and moves the object a single space. Once you spend the Entanglement needed to start levitating the object, you can continue to move it at a rate of one space per round with no additional Entanglement cost until you use any action other than continuing to move it, or until you lose concentration. Once you use another action or lose concentration the object falls to the ground if you have not set it down already.

This style also allows you to make kinetic attacks as a combat action with a speed cost of 6 and an Entanglement cost of 1. These attacks can target anyone within a range of twice the number of ranks you have in this style. Kinetic attacks deal 1 die of damage per rank you have in this style and automatically hit a target in the bodily location you choose. When using a kinetic attack, you lift a small object (no more than a kilogram) with your mind and send it speeding into the target. Doing so requires that there be small unattended objects in the target's surroundings that can be used as projectiles. Most environments have enough small objects to do normal damage to the target, although at the GM's discretion some environments may not contain small objects to use, or only contain blunt objects that would deal unarmed damage(and thus be able to be resisted by a Stamina roll). Kinetic attacks deal damage to those protected by the function shield( ) or the style Retrogradient at the same rate as Firearms or Ballistics attacks.

Tier 1: Redirect Momentum

Rank 1: You can spend a point of Entanglement outside of combat to gain the ability to sense minute details about the mass, density, momentum, and potential energy of your surroundings for the rest of the scene. While this ability is active you are aware of any motion in your surroundings even if you normally could not see or hear it. Because of this sensitivity to motion, you always know the exact location of all opponents in combat and you can always act in any surprise round while this ability remains active.

Rank 2: You can spend a point of Entanglement for each kilogram an object weighs to start levitating it.

Rank 3: You can manipulate the inertia of any small (no more than a few kilograms) moving object, causing it to accelerate, slow down, or change direction. This is a full action that costs 3 points of speed and a point of Entanglement. Alternatively, you can also use this action to manipulate the trajectory of incoming ranged attacks, for the rest of the round you may spend 1 point of speed each time you are targeted with a ranged attack to cause the attack to miss.

Tier 2: Inertial Kinesis

Rank 4: You must spend a point of Entanglement for every 10 kilograms the object weighs to start levitating it.

Rank 5: You can manipulate the momentum of your own body enough to double the number of successes you get on all Climb, Jump, and Traverse actions. This benefit always applies to you, but also you can grant it to an adjacent ally for the rest of the round by spending a point of Entanglement.

Rank 6: You must spend a point of Entanglement for every 30 kilograms the object weighs to start levitating it.

Tier 3: Mass Kinesis

Rank 7: You can manipulate the momentum of your own body so that whenever you sprint as a full round action, double the number of spaces you can cross. This benefit always applies to you, but you can grant it to an adjacent ally when they start a sprint action by spending a point of Entanglement. Additionally, your kinetic attacks deal damage to those protected by shield( ) or Retrogradient as if they were melee attacks.

Rank 8: You must spend a point of Entanglement for every 300 kilograms the object weighs to start levitating it.

Rank 9: You can manipulate the inertia of a large (no larger than a full-size van) moving object, causing it to accelerate, slow down or change direction. This is a full action that costs 6 points of speed and a point of Entanglement. Alternatively, you can also use this action to manipulate the trajectory of incoming ranged or melee attacks, for the rest of the round you may spend 1 point of speed each time you are targeted with a ranged or melee attack to cause the attack to miss.
